The Department of life sciences and facility management (school) of the ZHAW is a national centre of excellence relating to nutrition, health, society and the environment. The school commissioned IAM Institute of applied media science to produce a strategic communication approach. Starting an identity and reputation analysis, a communication strategy is developed and light in the form of a communication-roof concept written. Based on the structures, processes, and tasks of Department - Institute communication are regulated. Project management: Prof. Dr. Nicole Rosenberg, head of research in organizational communication and management, IAM. Project collaboration: Markus Nagar, Thomas Gantenbein.
