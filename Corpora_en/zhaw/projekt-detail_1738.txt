This study is a part of the long-term to IAM project Absolvententracking. It provides insights into the requirements of the practice of graduates and graduates of journalism and organisational communications. While the study focuses on expected skills and basic skills, as well as factors in personality. The study is divided after the two disciplines to help determine both common and different qualification requirements. The results can be recycled in the assessment, as well as lesson plans, and curriculum. The results are incorporated in a regular quantitative Graduate Survey (project Absolvententracking).
