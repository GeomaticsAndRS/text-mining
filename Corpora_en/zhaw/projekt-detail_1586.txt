On behalf of the city of Winterthur, three colleges accompany a participative development project in the Winterthur district Töss. It comes in close cooperation with the local population to maintain the quality of life in Töss and increasing. To various large group events were carried out, and 7 thematic working groups worked for six months. A program is developed from the numerous projects in the implementation phase for Töss, that shall be implemented by local authorities and population. The entire process is evaluated, and the universities create a guide for the transfer of knowledge in other local communities.
