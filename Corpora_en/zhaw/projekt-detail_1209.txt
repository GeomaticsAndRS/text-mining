The optimization of customer relationships is an absolute top subject in Swiss companies. The ZHAW-CRM framework understands CRM as enterprise-wide integrated management principle. It shows the 10 areas of expertise, a comprehensive customer relationship management. The CRM framework is the backbone of the CRM lessons, and is also used by numerous companies and consulting firms used in the practice. All areas of customer relationship management play an important role for any company. On the different business situations to be able to customize the existing CRM framework, a new CRM-E-learning tool was developed. A questionnaire, in which the relevant criteria for the various components of the ZHAW CRM framework be questioned is based on this tool. The weighted responses allow you to identify the relevant CRM fields of expertise and to create a CRM framework specifically tailored to the business situation. So are appropriate measures and resources are used more efficiently.
