Gentinetta Scholten the focus of research in organizational communication and public at the IAM Institute of applied Media Sciences cooperates with Gentinetta * Scholten Zurich, a Swiss company for socio-political consulting (Dr. Katja Gentinetta, Heike Scholten, M.A.). He developed an analysis of the public discourse in the policy field of Swiss social security in this context.
