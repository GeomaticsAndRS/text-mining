The project is the construction and operation of a research group KOMPAZ competence centre on Lake Zurich to the target. This group forms an interchange and contact platform for all actors around Lake Zurich (technical and cross-border, also outside), develops visionary and holistic concept ideas, builds a knowledge management for the Lake (detects and fixes knowledge deficits and makes knowledge accessible), developed concepts, documented the development in the and Lakeside, informed and sensitized the population for the concerns of the Lake Habitat for man and nature.
