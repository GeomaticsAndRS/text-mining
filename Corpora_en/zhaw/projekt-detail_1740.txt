Withdrawal from the area offers the natural, unoccupied landscape no economic livelihoods in a division of labor economy/society. The traditional land use by the agriculture produces a relatively low value. Agriculture can offer specifically no livelihood than conventional economic usage due to the economic conditions - in 'disgrace layers' - without sideline for the economic operators. Also, the extensive use of tourism reach profitability limits. Innovative new forms of use be encouraged and promoted. New offerings especially the leisure industry aiming to generate more value added on a smaller area. The module 'Withdrawal from the area' want to watch current recreational land use developments and anticipate future developments. Using a typing of various forms of use (tourist, 'virtual') to estimate, what added values are reached. Target is the comparative representation of various uses in the form of a diagram.
