In the investigation is the question addressed, how social differences affect the handling of demented relatives and relations with professional support systems. 12 caring (-) spouses and partners from four different regions of the social space on the basis of an interview are asked on the basis of a qualitative research design. With the chosen theoretical frame of reference the position-specific problems and logics of dealing to collect differentiated with demented members as is the case with conventional layer models. Based on the obtained results offers relief can be institutionalized, which meet the needs of different Social Milieus and strengthen their specific problem coping resources, so that people with dementia can remain as long as possible in their usual family environment. Project end: 29.2.2008
