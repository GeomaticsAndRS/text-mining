The engineers and architects of the section of FRP of the Institute for structural design (IKE) at the Department invited the construction industry to the third symposium "Composites in construction" architecture, design and civil engineering on the 26 March 2014. The 130 participants received up-to-date information on building with fiber composites (FRP) in this one-day event. Internationally renowned speakers showed applications for fibre-reinforced plastics on the basis of running structures in supporting applications and presented projects and design manual. In the accompanying exhibition presented your products trade companies and were interested participants with their specialists. For the first time also a textile-reinforced concrete were at this meeting and presented concrete reinforced with carbon.
