The OEC compass is a free online research tool for operation and Volkswirtschafter students. An online search tool is provided to students of business and economics, with which they are efficiently to high-quality information in printed publications and on the Internet. Other target groups are students of law and social sciences. Since the website is free to the public, benefit even more interested (such as journalists, researchers, public authorities). The implementation of the paper on the e-learning version was funded by the BBT in the framework of competition creatools@UAS. The thematic update happens in preparation for teaching at the ZHW.
