The Pilatus is a diverse game room that must meet many demands on the use of space. A solving-oriented wildlife management requires that the development of wild stocks with sufficient reliability is known. Statistics on casualities and hunting routes, and counts reflect only an incomplete picture of the actual situation. On behalf of the Department of fishing and hunting of the canton of Lucerne, chamois, roe deer, red deer and their stock and hunting routes development will be investigated the Habitat situation of wild ungulates on the Luzern page of Pilate (wild Room 5).
