To predict Accarda AG to the reaction of a clientele on promotional activities, 3 different sets of variable available 10, 30 and 52 explanatory variables. The most telling data source is to be determined by analytical methods. On each of these three sets, a predictive variable combination determined and carried out a forecast of the customer response with data mining tools such as classification trees, random forests, support vector machines, and logistical classifiers. The found variable sets, as well as the various forecasting methods by means of specially designed lift were judged charts and associated confidence intervals for their predictive utility then graphically displayed.
