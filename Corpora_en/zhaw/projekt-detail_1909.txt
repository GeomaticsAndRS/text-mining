In cooperation with the industry partner Sulzer Medica (later centerpulse, today rooms) methods were developed, to grow three-dimensional cartilage tissue from autologous cartilage cells in a special reactor systems. The high quality of this Knorpelkonstrukte managed through the development of suitable methods of analysis to prove. This justifies the clinical use such cartilage implants for the regeneration of cartilage defects in joints, such as in patients with sports injuries
