The project NOVA (new online management departments) is an internal school of management and law (SOM) project and involves the creation of a centre cross-administrative software as a tool for the Department. The application is based on a Web interface (Flex 4.0) and the architecture is built on SOA principle. Following ways available to legitimate users: - organisation of personnel - acquisition and management of projects - acquisition and management of training offerings - planning by entering SOLL-Werten and control by entering the values - reporting using PDF exports
