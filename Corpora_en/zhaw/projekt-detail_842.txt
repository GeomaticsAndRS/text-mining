It creates a comprehensive and updated mass flow analysis (MFA) of the rivers of biogenic goods of Switzerland based 2009 on the data collection year. The data structure of the study for the year 2006 is taken as far as possible. In the area of digestion is a further refinement of the structure: digesters of ARA, agricultural biogas plants, industrial bio-gas plants and anaerobic industrial wastewater treatment should be a distinction made here. Analog to the study "Biogenic good rivers of Switzerland 2006" be that visualizes the results in the form of in - and output graphics by Sankey diagrams and also shown in tabular form. Mass flows are the reference unit in dry matter (TS). The presented results are explained for each process and commented. To specify the data sources in a bibliography.
