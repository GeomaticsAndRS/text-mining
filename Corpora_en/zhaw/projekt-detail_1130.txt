Youth and career guidance of the canton Zurich, the study focuses on the following key research questions: • what protective factors contribute to successful adoption and what risk factors to their failure? • Interact as the protective and risk factors in the process of adoption? What is • a potential for improvement in the context of clarification (forecast) and permit? The focus of the project is set, that findings with regard to the licensing procedure of the central adoption and on the progress of adoptions in the canton of Zurich can be expected; with the best interests of the child as target priority. This focus includes also general recommendations for the foster child range, because many Strukturlogiken and the resulting problems have a comparability.
