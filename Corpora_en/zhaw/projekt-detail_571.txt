FHNW: social planning urban development the planning software Indosity of the Swiss company Tsquare and Urbanis to be further developed into a specialized tool for sustainability-oriented residential development. To do this, the social dimension as a planning base should be exposed as a supplement to building basics. Indosity should then allow to edit so-called "meaning cards" parallel to the traditional zones and cadastral plans. The town of Langenthal is used as a case study.
