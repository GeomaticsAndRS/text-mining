An interdepartmental platform is created with the establishment of a social network, which combines technical and engineering scientific themes with social issues. ZHW's strategic objectives of Mehrspartigkeit interdisciplinary link in this network. So will the interdepartmental Exchange and cooperation will be encouraged in terms of content, structure and personnel.
