The supervised youth living communities are a measure of residential integration of Zurich for young adults with no or unclear daily structure. There are 28 places in several apartments available. The young men and women are cared for down-threshold during 24 hours. Purpose of stay is the promotion of social and personal skills, as well as finding a solution in the area of housing. In the framework of the evaluation is focused on the effects of the offer. The findings are based on interviews with young people, social workers, as well as on the analysis of files and a group discussion. The project was completed at the end of June 2012.
