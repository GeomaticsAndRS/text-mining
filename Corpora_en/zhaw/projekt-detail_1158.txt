The journalistic coverage of mental illness and the mentally ill is investigated. Posts in 19 daily newspapers of Switzerland, Germany and Austria are based on the analysis. The sample of daily newspapers covering different reporting styles and different journalistic performance. So put together, the sample allows a restrained conservative conclusion on the whole of journalistic services on the subject of mental illness. The posts are selected by means of selected tags. Investigation period is the year 2008. Qualitative and quantitative aspects, but especially the journalistic resources which qualify mental illness and the mentally ill are analyzed. Goal is to realize a reliable inventory of reporting and to generate practical guidance for journalists from the findings. These should be diffused by means of workshops and brochures in the practice.
