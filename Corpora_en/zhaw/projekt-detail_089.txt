The selection of apprenticeships is great. Nevertheless, you must opt for a more or less preferred profession - usually several available options. How should you decide? Against this background, the basis for a work module will be developed which supports such decisions. E.g. decision theories are accompanying concepts in the development of this means of work, considerations of sex type of occupations, to the prestige of professional titles, to the role of social origin, family and peers. The objective is to edit conflict areas with the help of the working fluid for the career choice and to enable a decision on appropriate type and evaluate.
