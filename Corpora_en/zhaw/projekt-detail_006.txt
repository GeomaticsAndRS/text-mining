In the funding programme of the international of German Teacher Association (IDV), a D-A-CH regional studies seminar (Germany Austria Switzerland and Liechtenstein if necessary) takes place every two years. Twenty teachers of German from different state associations of the IDV experiencing the peculiarities and differences of the language-cultural, regional and economic-historical peculiarities of the three participating countries by their stay in the three countries and carry this information as home to their countries of origin. Practical geography becomes a vehicle for understanding of values, settings and forms of behaviour in intercultural exchange - and of course for the consolidation of the main varieties of German. During the seminar, the participants of their own national research and project work on selected topics take over. You use their different ideas and methods for the foreign language classes, develop educational applications of geography in collaboration for the own school - and training lessons and discuss implementation options for a successful transfer to their countries of origin.
