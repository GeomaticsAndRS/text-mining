Aimed at a knowledge transfer between research, authorities and with the patient will be working Medizinalpersonen in the field of phytotherapy. The transfer is carried out within the framework of an international conference with lectures, posters, field trips and a social program. The target audience is primary in the German-speaking world. The meeting will be every two years alternately in de, OE and CH. First implementation in the Switzerland. For 10 years the ZHAW/FG, phytotherapy organized a Swiss annual meeting.
