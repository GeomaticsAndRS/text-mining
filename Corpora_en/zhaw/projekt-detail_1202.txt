Armasuisse wants a situation assessment system for your ICT infrastructure and the adjacent processes of the army. The ZHAW showed this with IBM in theory, how such a system could be established in principle. To implement the system, a scenario was designed and implemented in a demonstrator. It the analysis of the data obtained with the demonstrator, the extension of the demonstrator with other scenarios, as well as the concrete implementation of the position evaluation system are now in further steps
