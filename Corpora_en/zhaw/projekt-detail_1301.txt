At the by the ZHAW together with the sanu, Biel is all 2 years organized meeting for all involved to the professional training and networking opportunity offered. End of March 2009 the second national forum on environmental management was held in Sursee. Applications in practice, as well as innovations in the legal field have been shown. The focus topics of alpine and urban construction sites were almost 160 participants to a deeper insight into this complex topic. The constructive dialogue between those responsible in the areas of planning, construction, environment, and the authorities could be strengthened on the Forum and expanded mutual understanding.
