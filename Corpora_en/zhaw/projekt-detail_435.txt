Club unternehmenGOMS on the example of the energy region of Goms, VS, are foundations for understanding and for making the acceptance as well as the spatial / meteorological, technical and economic limits and possibilities of new (small) wind turbines is collected. An exemplary win-win situations are pointed out, which allow a socially acceptable increase in renewable energy production. Following institutes and centers within the school of engineering cooperate interdisciplinarily: Institute for sustainable development (project management), Institute for energy systems and fluid engineering, Centre for product and process development, Centre for aviation. The interdisciplinary nature of the project is given by the collaboration of engineering, natural and social sciences centres and institutes. While both technical questions and social issues to the acceptance in a specific environment (tourism region) and economic issues are examined.
