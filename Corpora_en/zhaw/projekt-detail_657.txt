Förderverein CoCoMo Jucomo is an offer of the Förderverein CoCoMo. It is aimed at recognised refugees in the age between 15 and 25 years (residence permit F B), temporarily captured foreigners and foreigners (status F), as well as migrants and migrants. The evaluation of Jucomo focus in particular the effects of the offer. This will be collected from the perspective of participants, the involved companies and referring agencies. Qualitative interviews with participating, telephone interviews with the involved companies as well as a survey of companies and allocated points allow a multi-perspective approach to effects. Also is a file analysis the question addressed what works under what conditions, i.e. what interventions by Jucomo and which biographical constellations of the participants determine what effect.
