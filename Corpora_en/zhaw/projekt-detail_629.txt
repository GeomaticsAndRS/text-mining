Urban models can form a framework for the spatial development of the districts. Until now lacking a refined and implementation-oriented scheduling method for their preparation. The aim of the research project is to determine a planning method for the development of urban district development models based on knowledge of theory and practice, and to test on the basis of two case studies. The innovation lies in the project is that allow by the Institute urban planning method to developing the Department of social work and landscape combines Interdisciplinary content such as urban studies, planning theory, planning, and participatory processes. The result of the project will allow the cities of Zurich and Winterthur and the planning offices involved, more efficiently to develop neighbourhood development models, social anchor and sustainably implement their content.
