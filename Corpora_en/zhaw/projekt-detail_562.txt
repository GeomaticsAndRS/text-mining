For the International Office of the ZHAW School of management law, an intranet-based application procedure for students who are interested is currently developing on a semester abroad at partner universities. The project aims at students individually and comprehensively on a semester abroad at partner institutions appropriate for them to inform and animate through a simplified application process for increased participation. At the same time, also a simplification of the process flow for the staff at the International Office when you edit the application applications is achieved by the Web application. The development follows agile methodologies (Scrum, kanban) incrementally and iteratively in close consultation with the customer. It is only (license-free) open source software used which reflects the current state of the art (alternative: represents) (including MVC web framework, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3).
