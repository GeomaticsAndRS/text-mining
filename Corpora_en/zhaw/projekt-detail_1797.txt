In the innovation project pitched roof, the Center focuses with design and construction questions of the sloped roof structural design ZKE in research and teaching. As fields of research are focused its options with regard to contextual insertion, embossed design structural and spatial effect. In the Centre stands the interaction of form, space, light, use, structure and construction. Aims at exploring the obvious and hidden potentials of the steep roof, which will be explored and evaluated in different courses and modules. If necessary, they can evolve in collaboration with industry partners to innovative, constructive principles or systems. Try project were defined on the basis of a first inspection and analysis phase. Three of these projects are currently in progress: pitched roof increases, steep roof monolith and pitched roof manual.
