The study aims to develop of new methods for the environmental management. A drone, which is intended to provide images of actually claimed perimeter as well as knowledge of the incurred damage is used for this. In addition, the study deals with eco-friendly implementation of demolitions and new construction of facilities relating to the Ski World Cup 2017. The aim is to ensure compliance with the legal requirements for environmental protection in construction projects. A care is taken during the construction phase on the greatest possible protection of nature and landscape, soil and waters. Special attention is given here to the alpine habitats.
