The aim of this project is to develop of an applicable in radio newsrooms instrument for the purpose of controlling internal program. A practical instrument in the hand should be given editorial executives, which used for programme monitoring and evaluation (public performance) - sets can be, the internal criticism and feedback culture promotes and supports the editorial leadership in the development of the programme strategy. The goal of application-oriented research project consists in the empirically-supported development of a practicable monitoring instrument, of Redaktionsverantwortlichen in the area of radio for on set can be systematically and continuously to evaluate the own programmes. .
