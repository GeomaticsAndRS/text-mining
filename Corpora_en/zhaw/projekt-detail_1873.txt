In this E-learning project, a german English WBT course is designed for students in the first year of the degree of translation. The course is application-oriented knowledge of the use of various convey in translation practice of basic tools and thus promote the instrumental professional competence of students as well as support acquiring another translational key competences (transfer competence, strategic competence) for other courses of the programme significantly. The aim is a modular course design, which allows a combination of behaviorist, kognitivistischer and above all constructivist teaching and learning approaches and at the same time meets the level of knowledge of individual students. The project was successfully completed on the 1.4.2003. The course was first implemented in the winter semester of 2002/03.
