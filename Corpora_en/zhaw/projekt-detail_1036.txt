The project salute is a network of volunteers for the accompaniment of people, both health and social problems are concerned. Salute is part of the health network 2025 of the city of Zurich and is supported by the src of the canton of Zürich and the Stadtärztlichen service. The clients and clients of salute can be assigned to by primary care physicians and other institutions of the health and social services of a Coordination Office. Four studies scientifically examines the impact of the programme. At the beginning and at the end of intervention measurements on the clients and clients take place. It is carried out also a follow-up, a qualitative analysis of the case, as well as a comparison with the project compass of the health network by 2025.
