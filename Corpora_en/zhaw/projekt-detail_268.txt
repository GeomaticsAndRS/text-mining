Soreco AG aim of this empirical study is to collect the diffusion and maturity of business process management in organizations in the German-speaking world and to show their development since 2011. On the one hand new insights on the State of the IT support of BPM to be won. On the other hand will determine which trends for organizations of from diverse industries are currently or in the near future of interest. This study is intended to provide cross-industry and industry-specific statements. A special but not exclusive focus is on the real estate industry and it-specific processes.
