The clinic has reorganized in 2003 the working time rules. A survey in the spring 04 will be where the employees are the new regime after the introduction and how they change have experienced and implemented investigated. To do this, discussions are led and qualitatively evaluated. In addition to quantifiable data are collected with a written survey (survey). The project is one part of the CTI of research financed with and development project. Five case studies patterns of individual processing of change are exemplary, examines the management of change and communication in the process of change.
