Project completed. Valid since 2011 care law of the canton of Zurich, the municipalities are obliged to carry out a material requirements planning in the field of long term care. The Department of health will help the communities this task, providing an instrument for material requirements planning available. The objective of the study is to develop the methodological and substantive basis for an instrument for the planning of long-term care sites in the canton of Zurich. To do this, first collected influence factors of long-term care with a systematic literature review. In a next step established records obtained, which can be used for the estimation of demand. Finally, statistical forecasting models are proposed, which can estimate the future demand for beds in long-term care.
