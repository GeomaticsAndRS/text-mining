In cooperation with the regional nature park of Schaffhausen and Schaffhauserland tourism is developed a concept to the nature - and culture-oriented tourism in the canton of Schaffhausen. It shows up as a high-quality and sought after range line in the nature - and culture-oriented tourism sector can be rebuilt. Also, a guide will be developed, which serves as a practical instrument of support when developing offer in nature - and culture-oriented tourism the tourist providers and service providers.
