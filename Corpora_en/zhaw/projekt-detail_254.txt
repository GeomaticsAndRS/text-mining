Not on tagbare diseases (NCDs) are responsible in advanced countries for the vast majority of mortality, morbidity and healthcare costs. Objective of the study is the calculation of the direct medical costs of NCDs in the Switzerland, as well as the calculation of indirect costs, which arise because of illness-related work. The study will be conducted on behalf of the Federal Department of health in cooperation with the Institute for social and preventive medicine of the University of Zurich and the consultancy firm of Polynomics.
