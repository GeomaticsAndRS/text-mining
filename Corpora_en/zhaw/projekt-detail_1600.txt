A "Pooling of public real estate" investment vehicles for pension plans should be developed and then successfully placed on the market within the framework of the project. Economic efficiency gains resulting, by parts of the public real estate are newly subject to the market mechanism. The ZHW is responsible for the research activities with regard to violations of market potential (supply and Nachfrageseitig), as well as the calculation of the return risk profile of real estate investments. The legal aspects, the distribution and the portfolio management in the ANLA-gevehikels is the main economic partner.
