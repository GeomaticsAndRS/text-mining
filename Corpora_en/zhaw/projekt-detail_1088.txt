Mobile communications media open up new horizons for a variety of applications. The planned basic application for mobile devices is used as a starting point and framework for a variety of internal and external applications with regard to the communication of information in nature. Each Department can use this application and continue to develop for their own purposes. For the vegetation analysis departments and environmental planning, it is particularly interesting to provide information on forest edges. Keys of types of and valuation methods can be arranged easily. The tool can be arbitrarily expanded via extensions. So a wide variety of applications for any sites should be based on the planned application possible. Species key for flora and fauna, identification keys are possible for vegetation, rocks, etc. The implementation of enlargement, as well as their costs will be taken over by the departments.
