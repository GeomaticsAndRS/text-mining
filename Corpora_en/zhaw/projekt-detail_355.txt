The Rhaetian Railway (RhB) must refurbish the backrest viaduct Foura d ' Valorga in Ardez, which was built in 1911 and commissioned in 1913. It was applied for the use of environmental management. The environmental management aims on the one hand, compliance with the legal requirements for environmental protection in the construction projects. On the other hand it provides suitable measures for maximum protection of nature and landscape, soil and waters during the construction phase.
