Constantly introduced new pests and diseases in the Switzerland. To take the best action, the Schadbilder must be recognized correctly. A plant protection course with fresh gangrenous plant material and for other practical exercises at the disposal is made for training purposes. Every participant has to assess the opportunity in smaller groups the Schadbilder (E.g. box-tree borer, citrus Longhorn Beetle. etc.). Quarantine organisms are explained on the basis of good picture material. Latest information from research and development will be presented.
