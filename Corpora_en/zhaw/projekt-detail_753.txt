Coordination Group on youth violence of the canton of Zurich the project examines the need for preventive measures in the area of violence and other problem behavior in adolescents and young adults in Zurich communities. A qualitative assessment of the needs of community-oriented prevention work in the area of youth violence and other problem behaviours such as alcohol and drug use, vandalism, waste is carried out on the basis of a written survey of municipal councils, as well as in-depth interviews with experts. Project end: 31.8.2011
