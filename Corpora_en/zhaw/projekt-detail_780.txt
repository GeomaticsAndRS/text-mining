The Valedo ™ motion is a medical therapy system for the treatment of back pain by using functional, entertaining and motivational exercises. Valedo ™ based on inertial sensors for measuring motion translational and Rotary acceleration forces during back movement. Movement laboratory of the Institute of physiotherapy, the measurement results of Valedo ™ motion were compared with an optical measuring system (Vicon ®). 10 healthy people led different test movements and were measured at the same time with both systems. Both systems showed a high match here in the primary direction of movement. The project was successfully completed.
