Given of increasing urbanization, ecological and social demands of urban green spaces grow Association Swiss city nurseries and horticulture departments. Moreover, rising cost pressures with regard to the management. In this project, the label green city will be developed Switzerland. Cities and towns are awarded the label, who want sustainable planning their City Green according to defined and measurable criteria, build and manage, and communicate these services to population and politics. The project will take place in cooperation with the Association of Swiss city nurseries and horticulture departments VSSG, the Research Institute of organic agriculture FiBL, Bioterra and the city gardeners Basel, Lucerne and Winterthur.
