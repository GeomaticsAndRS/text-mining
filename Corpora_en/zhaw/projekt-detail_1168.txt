As urban sprawl continues to increase in the Swiss plateau, the open green belt between the municipalities of periurbanen gaining - mainly as a recreational area and identifying spaces. Decision makers will be increasingly required in the future to negotiate over local in their spatial and landscape planning. To help them, in the present project as instruments developed a standing and a presentation of arguments, they tested in a participatory process with decision makers and applied to a case study. The project is in cooperation with the canton of Aargau, upper Freiamt and the municipalities of Muri, Menrenschwand and Benzenschwil realised region. It was completed in 2011.
