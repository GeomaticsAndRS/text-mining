IAM researches for the national policy of prevention IAM researches on behalf of the Federal Department of health, Department of national prevention programmes (NPP) public discourse to the policy of prevention in the Switzerland. Target is to identify of important patterns of public production of meaning (narrative and key arguments) by the various actors in this policy field. The Department is responsible for prevention programs in the areas of nutrition and movement, drugs, tobacco and alcohol. The project is headed by the Professor of organizational communication and public (Prof. Dr. Peter Santos Herlach).
