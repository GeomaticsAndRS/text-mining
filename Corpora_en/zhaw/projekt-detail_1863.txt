Monitoring of media services at the theming of risks of electro magnetic fields (EMF) objective: development of a monitoring system for the analysis and interpretation of media coverage when the theming of EMF risks. The monitoring system examined the attention structures, selection, processing, information - and presentation processes of the Swiss-German (print) media system on the subject of EMF and thus gives clues about the success of the monitoring - and credibility strategies of actor categories participating in public discourse. Method: quantitative content analysis client: research cooperation of sustainable mobile communication at ETH Zurich
