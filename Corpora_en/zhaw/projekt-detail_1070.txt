The IAM research requirements, characteristics, forms and instruments of the com munikationsleitung, i.e. a professional action, which focuses on the strategic management of organizational communication. Secondary analyses form the basis for the development of a framework, which is the education and training modules of the IAM in the field of organizational communication based papers and industry reports and interviews with experts. The project is led by the professors of organizational communication, Nicole Rosenberger and Peter Santos Herlach.
