L of the ZHAW aims to develop of a common European framework of reference for sign languages (GER GS). In a first step, to the existing teaching material for the German Swiss studied sign language (DARIA) with regard to their suitability for teaching and it practically and systematically catalogued. While several deaf project co-workers are trained to qualify them for the upcoming research in a SNF follow-up project. The more extensive research is needed to identify the existing gaps in the curricula, to close and to develop appropriate descriptors for a GER for sign languages. (End 31.10.2012)
