The ZHAW Center analyzes funds of hedge funds (FoHF) for institutional investors on the basis of hedgegate (www.hedgegate.com). The selection of Fund of hedge funds is based on by 3 steps. 1 performance rating: on the basis of the performance available on hedgegate rating is a long list created. 2. the risks of the Fund of hedge funds on the long list will be by means of two factor models in terms of style risks and analyze market factor risk. 3. the final selection analysis the correlations between the Fund of hedge funds. In the future, also the tail dependencies are analyzed.
