GDK East / Canton of Glarus a majority of the Eastern Swiss cantons confirmed its interest in the topic of kantona-le/regional health insurance fund and decides the order of studies at the Winterthur Institute for health economics. Today's system with a variety of health insurance companies and possible improvements are subjected to objective analysis. The model of a kan tonal or regional health insurance company is compared with the current health insurance system. The study results are to be expected year end.
