Complementa investment controlling AG project "ratings for funds of hedge funds (FoHF)" developing methods for a rating of Fund of hedge funds, which is tailored to this asset class. In a first phase, a rating is developed, on the one hand based on the yields of individual Fund of hedge funds, and on the other hand to compare with relevant groups of Fund of hedge funds (peers). In a second phase, procedures are developed for the measurement of operational risk. The results of both phases is then incorporated into a comprehensive rating system for Fund of hedge funds.
