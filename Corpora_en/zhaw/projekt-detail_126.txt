Federal Office of roads the aim of the research project is to develop, the 1st road users support and at the same time helps 2 in his choice of routes, to implement the objectives of the traffic management a solution proposal for a standardized travel time information (MIV). The travel time information on Exchange text messages (variable message signs) should be quickly perceived by road users and collected. The related distraction from the surrounding traffic should be as low as possible. A standardization of the presentation of information supports a quick perception of the habituation effect. The result is the road operators able to obtain a best possible steering effect and at the same time not unnecessarily to endangering the safety of road users.
