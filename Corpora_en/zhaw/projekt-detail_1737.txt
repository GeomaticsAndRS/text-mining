The Swiss Bankers Association was faced with the challenge to make the training services in the field of commercial vocational training more efficient and cost-conscious. The ZHCM was first entrusted with a preliminary study and then was commissioned to develop the business case for a total Swiss competence centre within the framework of a feasibility study, the Center for Young Professionals in banking. In just four months, succeeded together with the core team of the customer from the still very open and partly controversial starting point to develop an Association political consensus enabled, didactic sustainable and economically viable concept. In the critical examination of the individual banks, which faced its own internal business cases to the case, the assumptions and conditions proved sufficiently detailed so that could be developed on the basis of the business case is also equal to the business plan.
