In this E-learning project, the Institute of language in business and education (ISBB) created 50 to 60 interactive exercises for DaF (German as a foreign language) learners on level B2/C1. The product is to be sold later in cooperation with a publishing house as a DVD. With challenging texts and audio files, reading and listening skills improved, refined style and grammar and the vocabulary expands. The innovative didactic exercises are deposited with specific feedback and increase learning efficiency. Should be taken into account equally different language skills and individual types of learners. So the interactive teaching material for the target group is easy to use, attractive and efficient learning, test and evaluate DaF students at the ISBB ongoing individual parts of the resulting product. The project is supported by the CSPC (competence, service, production centre of the Zurich University of applied sciences) with a larger sum and accompanied.
