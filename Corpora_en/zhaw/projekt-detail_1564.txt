School social work in the canton of Zurich is needed urgently increased coordination and control. This is reflected on the one hand because of development problems specifically could be observed. On the other hand, fundamental questions need to be addressed with regard to sponsorship and aims of school social work. The present versions are as a basis for the surveys planned in the period from Oct. 05 to March 06 in the canton. Objective of these surveys is to May 06 for the attention of the coordination of Canton of and control measures to propose.
