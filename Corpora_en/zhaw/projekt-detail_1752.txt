The Swiss Association was looking for banks with over six hundred branches a tailor-made solution to support the internal HR consultant in their performance effectively strengthen communication skills of internal consultant. On the basis of a thorough analysis of the situation with the customer, the ZHCM created a complete concept for internal training and realized this now with internal and external trainers. Special feature of the programme is highly action-oriented didactics. Challenges and problems of the consultants can be edited, this timely 1:1 in the workshops and the transfer problem (from theory to practice) is omitted here.
