The project «Views of space and time» wants to document the historical knowledge and therefore cultural heritage from the Surselva to management, culture, crafts, nature and social life for present and future generations and transmitted by means of innovative exhibition techniques in the context of current conditions and possibilities. At that time Vrin and Sumvitg should be with the help of event stations at various locations in and around Vals, experience. In this event stations, the so-called «viewpoints», then knowledge with stories of contemporary witnesses, images and interactive elements is made accessible a wide audience, without requiring it to transfigure the "good old days". A social discourse on the value of this knowledge for our future is initiated through this differentiated examination of the cultural heritage.
