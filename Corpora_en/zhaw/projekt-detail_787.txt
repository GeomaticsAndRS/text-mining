Teachers of German Goethe-Institut, Berlin by experienced geography learn close the peculiarities and differences of the language-cultural, regional and economic-historical peculiarities of Germany (Berlin), Austria (Vienna) and Switzerland (Zurich/Winterthur). The experiences allow them practical and action-oriented to make the teaching of German in their countries of origin. In the Swiss part of the three-week seminar took over the 25 participants of their own national research and projects on selected topics. The seminar was financially supported by the Goethe-Institut, Berlin. (Completion: July 2011)
