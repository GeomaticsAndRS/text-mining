A more efficient and competitive financial market based on similarly efficient institutions. The concept of efficiency or the measurement of the same are not always easy to implement in practice. So, for example, performance comparisons between banking institutions are often difficult to interpret, because certain factors (E.g., the size of the company) distort a direct comparison. Project benchmarking Club followed, from an economic perspective on the principle of the so-called value based management an approach and from methodological point of view, is based on a statistically based model for the explanation of a suitable performance measurement. Therefore, a meaningful basis of comparison between the Club members is aspired.
