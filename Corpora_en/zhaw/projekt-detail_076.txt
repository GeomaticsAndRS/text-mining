kybun AG the aim of this feasibility study is the identification of external conditions (E.g. geometry, surface, physical material properties,...), which increasingly enable the pelvic floor muscles while walking or standing. Based on this information, an insole is developed in a follow-up project, which in combination with a soft elastic shoe has a training effect on the pelvic floor muscles and can serve as a therapy approach to urinary incontinence for postnatal women.
