Swiss medical board the task of the Swiss Medical Board (SMB) is to analyze diagnostic procedures and therapist psychotherapeutic interventions from the point of view of medicine, economics, ethics, and justice. It is in the core to the assessment of the cost effectiveness ratio medical services, according to the criteria 'effective-practical economic"(WZW) of CCG. From this, recommendations are formulated for the attention of policymakers and the providers. The sponsorship of SMB allows now the impact of recommendations from two published reports ("rupture of the anterior cruciate ligament: operative or conservative treatment?";) "Significance of PSA levels in the early detection of prostate cancer") by the Winterthur Institute for health economics study as an example.
