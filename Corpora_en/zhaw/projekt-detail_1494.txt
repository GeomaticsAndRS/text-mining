The project connect has developed an overall concept in a 1st phase (2006-2008) for an integrated health care for the region of Winterthur. In a second phase (2009/2010) for selected topics of the overall design were plans business. The prepared foundations serve the implementation in phase 3 (since 2010). The project winner has received the award of the managed care forum in 2008. More information in the future at: www.gewint.ch
