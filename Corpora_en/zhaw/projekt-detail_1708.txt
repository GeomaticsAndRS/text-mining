The Swiss Bankers Association has the Center human capital management in the context of the establishment of its Center for Young Professionals in banking - a total Swiss competence centre for junior training - selected as a partner for the implementation of the recruitment. Due to the ambitious Foundation planning, management and the Board of education had to be recruited at short notice and selected. Here, the contracting authority placed great importance on the neutrality of the proceedings and on the comprehensive support in the selection process. The ZHCM carried out by the entire pre-selection on the basis of the written application documents and structured interviews based on the requirements, which had been developed in cooperation with the contracting authority. Thanks to the experience of the team could be complied with the demanding schedule and the entire procedure is completed in a few weeks.
