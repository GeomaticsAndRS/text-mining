In collaboration with the Department of marketing communication has developed the technical Office for new learning a betting game for the European Championship. The European Football Championship has started. Several weeks ago the students and staff of the ZHAW prepare for the event. The summer is all about the characters of football: each week a Panini picture Exchange takes place at the University. The game Italy against France on a big screen will be broadcast during the summer festival on June 17. The CENL designed in collaboration with the Department of online communication in the context of this euphoria a Predictor for the "SML International blog. We want to determine what members of the ZHAW in this area have the best idea. The tip King was allowed to celebrate his victory in the break even - with a drinks voucher sponsored by the school of management and law. The Predictor has implemented the CENL based on the open source PHP and MySQL technologies.
