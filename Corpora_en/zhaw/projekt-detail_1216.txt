Hofmann www.bhz.ch, Iseppi Kurath GmbH www.iseppi-kurath.ch in the study "energy-related modernisation of the Center da Capricorns / Hotel Piz Vizan - strategy of implementation methods" (G. Furger 2007) will develop the strategic direction for the implementation of renewable energy, implementing it described and made a first rough estimate. On this basis, following bases for the next planning stage (pre-project) are created in the project: - sustainability analysis of the proposed variants, proposal of an optimised solution - marketing concepts for "Renewable energy CdC" and review their potentials
