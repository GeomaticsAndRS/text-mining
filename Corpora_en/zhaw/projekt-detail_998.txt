The Subiaco people promotion carries out advertising campaigns in the whole Switzerland. For your CRM and employee management and operational planning is a database program based on MS Access, used. This database designed specifically for the Subiaco had some shortcomings were resolved in the framework of this project, and streamline processes. The deliverables included: - adaptation of the database structure and the surface for the display of images - creating a module for the automatic settlement of open positions - consistent implementation of corporate design on all reports - optimization of navigation for easy and intuitive operation - automation of selected processes (project closing etc.) - implementation of an automatic PDF generation of wage reports with E-mail client for sending auto - training
