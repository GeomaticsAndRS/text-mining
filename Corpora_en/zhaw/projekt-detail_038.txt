For the second time, IAM was commissioned by the Department of education of the canton of Zurich to evaluate the school journal. The survey of the target group provides hints for the use of the magazine, the acceptance, the assessment and the Act impacts associated with the recipient. In addition, it clarifies the expectations and interests. The survey was carried out online and delivered all cantonal hired teachers, headmasters, rectors of all levels as well as school President, school President of the Commission and of the members of the administrators of the canton of Zurich.
