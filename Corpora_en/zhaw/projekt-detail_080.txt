Sprachlehrinstitut SLI, Faculty of Philology, University of Freiburg The chair Language Competence and Knowledge Development has recently established a three-year inter-institutional collaboration with Sprachlehrinstitut (SLI), Faculty of Philology, University of Freiburg, in the area of English-medium Instruction starting in 2014. The areas of cooperation will include any program offered at either institution which is felt to promote the above-mentioned goals. The program in focus will be EMI (English Medium Instruction). The program should be of high priority for both parties. This program includes: a) joint research projects and workshops b) joint conferences c) exchange of staff d) exchange of publications
