The research group Wergenstein accompanied in the concept by 2013 formulated implementation ideas. Goal is a coordinated approach of the sub-regions in the Parc Adula in the valorisation of historic traffic routes. The analysis and selection of the ways is carried out on the basis of the scientific criteria of IVS (inventory historic traffic routes CH). The implementation of the project is done in collaboration with the person in charge of the Parc Adula. A documentation is posted on the approach.
