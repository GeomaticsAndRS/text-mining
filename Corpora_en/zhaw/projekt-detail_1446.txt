How is the Swiss Press Council in the newsrooms of media? Is the work of the Appeal Board enough perceived and used as tools in everyday work of media professionals? The research team of the IAM Institute of applied Media Sciences has to these and other questions on behalf of the Foundation Swiss Press Council a quantitative study conducted under the direction of Prof. Dr. Vinzenz Wyss. The online survey, stating that the reputation of the Swiss Press Council is very high. Generally, the Swiss Press Council as a professional is judged competent, practical and active. The opinions of the Press Council are discussed but hardly in the newsrooms. Editors with well integrated quality assurance system positively assess the Panel and its work and more often rely on the code and the guidelines.
