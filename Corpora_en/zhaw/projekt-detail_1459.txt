The creation process is an important factor, which can be observed in the creation of multilingual technical documentation, attached translation process. Because the processes during the translation process, as well as the tools used determine aspects of writing in the source language in regard to an optimized translation-oriented documentation. Vice versa, also the work processes are relevant during the preparation of documentation for translation workflow for quality increase and optimization of the economy. The project aims at providing a snapshot of the current processes and procedures at the interface of creation of documentation translation into Swiss companies, as well as an image of the medium-term objectives of the company in this area.
