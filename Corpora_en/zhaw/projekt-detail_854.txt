Back pain among the largest health and socio-economic issues trading AG, Winterthur in the Western industrialized nations. In addition to the high costs for the health system, this suffering is also limited therapy. The current therapeutic approaches such as back schools, manipulation and movement therapy show good short term effects and have shown how important is regular exercise. MBT uses a natural motion, going for therapeutic purposes. Study results show that training with the MBT shoe to a change in the motion during standing and walking, what in the long term can contribute to a change of the floor and Gehverhaltens in back pain patients. The aim of this study is to capture this change scientifically. The ZHAW movement laboratory offers an excellent way to measure the motion, the forces, as well as the muscle activity of selected trunk muscles and thus to study treatment effects of the MBT shoe and improve MBT. The project was successfully completed.
