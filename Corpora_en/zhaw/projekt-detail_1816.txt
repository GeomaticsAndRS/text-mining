The Department issued a new book to the technique and application of dry plaster building architecture, design and engineering of the Zürcher Hochschule Winterthur in cooperation with the Swiss Association of gypsum and gypsum board construction SVGG. Originally designed as a teaching tool, a comprehensive and clearly structured guide emerged from it, which imparts valuable basics and working tools in practice as well as in the education and training. Construction of à sec en plâtre - conception et réalisation. Publication of the English Edition (2005) this book was translated into French (2006).
