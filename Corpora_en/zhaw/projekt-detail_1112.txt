KSW Kantonsspital Winterthur in cooperation with IfP Institute for physiotherapy of the ZHAW analyzed the WIG the organizational structure and process, as well as the framework of physiotherapy of the KSW Kantonsspital Winterthur (phase 1: analysis) and an implementation plan for the future content and organizational design of this therapy facility of KSW created based on these results (phase 2: implementation planning). Currently, the results are implemented in the KSW.
