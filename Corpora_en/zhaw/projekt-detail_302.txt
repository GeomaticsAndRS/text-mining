Swissfundraising emerged in the last few years, that there is an asymmetry of information on employment and wage conditions in fundraising. The experiences of the Association Swissfundraising in the advice of members and other interested parties show that there is a need to know more to pay bandwidth. Also, the ZHAW as bearer of the diploma course in fundraising management recognizes this need for a recurring question for former students and employers is fundraising wages. Significantly improve the level of information of fundraising salaries of Association Swissfundraising intends to conduct a short survey with the ZHAW. Background of the cooperation between the ZHAW and the Association Swissfundraising is the jointly developed diploma of advanced studies (DAS) in fundraising management, offered to the professionalisation of the fundraising area since 2004.
