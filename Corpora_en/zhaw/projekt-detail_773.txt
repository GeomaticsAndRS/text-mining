The interpreting competence, less about the investigation of the then leading processes than about an inquiry into the subjectively perceived requirements should be pursued in addition to the translation project research (CTP project by Maureen honor Berger-Dow). The (perceived) competence development based on the statements of students is determined via questionnaires during the semester. In addition, professional interpreters are asked to work out differences between novices and experts in the approach to the capacity-building.
