In November 2011, the national experience exchange group formed a working group (erfa NHB), dealing with the social aspects of sustainable development within the framework of the NHB projects absorbed to the Nachhaltigkeitsbe assessment (NHB) of projects. In contrast to the ecological point of view the social aspects of sustainable development referred to in various documents are been underpinned with specific arguments nor validated by experts from the social sphere. This project aims to consolidate the social criteria suitable for the NHB projects, document and validate. To a commentary will explain the impact analysis "Social and human capital" and the criteria of Projektgouvernanz. Project objectives the report serves as a basis for NHB instruments and indicators systems in the social field. NHB-tools developers, NHB instruments users as well as sustainability experts of the Federal Government, the cantons and municipalities are target audience.
