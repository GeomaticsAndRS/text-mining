Drugs Forum train the Lüssihaus offers seven places with supervised daily structure (residential, working and social training) for drug-dependent people. The institution records that no abstinence is required. The inhabitants and residents are not willing or able to abandon their drug consumption. In addition to their addiction, they exhibit more psychiatric disorder or are aged at an early stage due to the consequences of their addiction, can not be but still in a nursing home. The line of Lüssihauses is attempting to align the institution as needed. People referring to as well as professionals in the field of addiction will be interviewed within the framework of a needs analysis. The results provide a basis for an adjustment and optimization of the offer as well as its concept.
