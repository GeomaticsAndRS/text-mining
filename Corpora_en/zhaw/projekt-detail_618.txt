: 01.01.2012 partners / project partner (s): expert group ag has the expert group ag in their division of the "expert and executive search" identifies a lack of good search solutions. Therefore the system "expert match" will be developed to the qualification of candidate profiles for the purpose of recruitment of highly specialized professionals. With the help of State-of-the-art information retrieval methods improves the search process of the expert group ag. The process is effective and efficient, so that both cost savings and a further increase of service quality can be achieved. The innovation lies in the application of specifically tuned IR methods on the present business process, as well as the parameterization and evaluation of the system.
