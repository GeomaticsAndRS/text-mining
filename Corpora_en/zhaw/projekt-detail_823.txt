The Centre Island Court is a centre of excellence for children and their families and offers a wide range of support services under one roof. With the aim to obtain evidence, as professionals know the offer of the Centre Island farm and how they evaluate their quality, Triemli was carried out on behalf of the Association Island Court a qualitative survey. Between November 2010 and February 2011 were questioned with 62 people who had made a request for a placement in the Centre Island Court in the course of the year 2010, by telephone. The answers to the formulated exclusively open questions were evaluated analytical content. The project was completed at the end of June 2011.
