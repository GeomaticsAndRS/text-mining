The SFOE 2000 conducted a comprehensive mass and energy flow analysis of biogenic goods for the Switzerland. This study is based on data current 1998 / 1999 2007 the FOEN has decided Federal Office for the environment in collaboration with the SFOE Swiss Federal Office of energy and the BLW Federal Office for agriculture, to bring the former study of the SFOE on the current state and to expand new aspects. The Department of environmental biotechnology of the ZHAW Zurich University of applied sciences is charged with carrying out this work.
