Article 106 of the Constitution regulates the operation of games of chance. As the executive body of the Federation the Federal game Bank (FGB) the implementation of this article of the Constitution Commission. Among others the approval of certain equipment than skill machines is located in its area of activity. In contrast to pure gambling machines they may be installed outside the venues (casinos), recognized by the Federal Government. In the framework of this project, the Institute for data analysis and process design was commissioned to review new slots for suitability as skill machines. For this purpose, player data collected and used multivariate statistical procedures, to examine key skill components such as experience and players effect.
