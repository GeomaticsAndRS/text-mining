In this project an eLearning course is developed signals and systems in the field, which can be used by the students via the Internet. Thematically, the course covers a basis trade, in the fields of electrical engineering, computer engineering, computer science and partly also mechanical engineering / energy technology and data analysis/process design is taught. The CBT course is an integral part of the training plans of appropriate fields of study at German universities with an annual graduate number of around 300 students.
