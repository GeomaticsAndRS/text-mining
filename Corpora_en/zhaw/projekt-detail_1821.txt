Access should be realised based on the remote access, as well as the Wi-Fi with Virtual Private Networks (VPN) IPSec. The corresponding tunnel connections are terminated on a Linux FreeS/WAN security gateway. After successful authentication on the basis of certificates is 509 user users granted full access to the ZHW network. It should be differed between students and ZHW staff (employees, assistants and lecturers). The second group is intended also to applications in the school network (such z.Bsp PACC and IAA) can be accessed.
