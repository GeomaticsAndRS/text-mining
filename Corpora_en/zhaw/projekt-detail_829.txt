FOEN, developed a framework of education on behalf of the Federal Office for the environment (FOEN) composed of concerned institutions and organizations, which meets the requirements of the education for sustainable development and lifelong learning. The framework describes the basics and current theoretical covers of contemporary environmental education education for parks and nature centers. It serves as planning, work and reflection instrument for education actors in parks and nature centers and more interested. As a framework, there is impetus to innovation and to the further professionalization of education in parks and nature centers. The project report was published no. 1220 in the FOEN environmental knowledge. Keywords: environmental education, education for sustainable development of GNI, lifelong learning, learning region, nature park, nature center, educational concept, framework training, instructional design, regional development
