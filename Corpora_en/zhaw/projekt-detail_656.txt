Focus work case management for the professional integration of provisionally admitted and recognised refugees is an offer of the company focus work. The aim is the social and professional integration of the mentioned target groups. The evaluation of case management focused in particular the effects of the offer. This will be collected from the perspective of participants, the involved companies and referring agencies. Qualitative interviews with participating, telephone interviews with the involved companies as well as a survey of companies and allocated points allow a multi-perspective approach to effects. Also is a file analysis the question addressed what works under what conditions, i.e. what interventions focus work and which biographical constellations of the participants determine what effect.
