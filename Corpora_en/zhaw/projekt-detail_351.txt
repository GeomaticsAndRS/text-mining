In the context of the present evaluation, subproject III were in the period from may until October 2013 sixteen potential and current employers interviewed (experts) in the German Switzerland by means of an interview guide. With the expert/internal survey was explored the practical relevance of the programme as the compatibility of the acquired competencies with the expectations of the employer. The interviews were held in the following professional fields: public authorities and administrative bodies, public social services, foundations/associations/clubs/NPOs as well as educational institutions.
