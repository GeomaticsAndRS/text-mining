For the hearing impaired, SWISS TXT began from autumn 2008 multiplied to provide television broadcasts with live subtitles, to dazzling spoken information as complete subtitles (= Respeaking). The Respeaking for as many programs of public television is required by the EU. This resulting in the need to explore aspects of Respeaking process and its reception to support barrier-free access to information. According to o.a framework describes orally also for visually impaired and blind film sections (audio description). Corresponding films have been bought so far almost exclusively abroad, new produced by Swiss television. Here are explored the claims of the target group, as well as to examine different production methods for audio description.
