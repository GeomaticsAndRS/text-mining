Social services and the social responsibility of companies increasingly attract the attention of the public and the legislative. Affected various corporate functions such as procurement (E.g. human rights, working conditions) may be, human resources (E.g., occupational safety, health, reconciling work and family life), but also sales and marketing (social product stewardship) etc. Social issues pose for businesses both opportunities and dangers. Until now approaches and instruments in Enterprise largely lacking, to capture social issues in their entirety, to check on their relevance and to integrate systematically in the management. Opportunities as well as dangers due to social issues are so often ignored. With this project, therefore in cooperation with company methods and instruments of corporate social management (-) developed and tested in practice. Goal is to complement that, social issues are systematically incorporated the existing management tools.
