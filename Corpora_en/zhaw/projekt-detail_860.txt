: 01.09.2010 partners / project partner (s): BPM Forum further information / further information: business process management (BPM) helps the analysis, modeling, implementation, execution and continuous improvement of business processes. Many companies and organizations seem the potential which BPM methodologies and solutions offer, still not fully to take advantage of, even if there is a general awareness. This empirical study shows the maturity and degree of diffusion, as well as timely and medium-term trends in German-speaking Europe. A total of 219 representatives from over 200 companies and institutions have taken part. The study reveals interesting new findings also critically to interpret aspects. Also provides information on issues relating to the current and future investment in BPM, as well as initiatives in the field of business process outsourcing (BPO).
