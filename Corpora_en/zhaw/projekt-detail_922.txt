The aim of the study is the analysis of journalistic production in cross-media-oriented editors compared to editors not involved in media-convergent environment. Editorial production and action sequences or routines and the journalistic values involved and standards are in the foreground. The study is based on the strukturationstheoretischen assumption that media change is determined not only by technological influences, but is taking place in a recursive process between technology and journalistic regulations. The results of the study will be before end of 2010.
