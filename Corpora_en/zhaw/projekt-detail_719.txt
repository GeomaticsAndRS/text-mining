First full cooperation of two ZHAW departments: ZHAW Health (Institute nursing, occupational therapy and physiotherapy) and ZHAW life sciences and facility management are addressed with the CAS to professionals from the health field, landscape architects and architects, environmental engineers and engineers. They learn professionally perform garden therapy interventions according to their educational background in interdisciplinary studies and to evaluate or plan gardens for therapeutic purposes. First successful implementation completed in March 2014. The next implementation takes place by 2015/16.
