Language barriers in the Spitex linguistic understanding and successful communication are of central importance for an efficient and effective nursing care. If patients due to limited German skills poorly with the nursing staff can communicate, known as language barriers exist. The maintenance process and establishing a professional relationship can be restricted what represents a burden for all concerned. To deepen the knowledge of language barriers in the context of the Spitexpflege, health and applied linguistics of the Zurich University of applied sciences (ZHAW) conducted at the departments an interdisciplinary study. This occurrence and extent as well as the consequences of language barriers are examined. The aim of the study is to determine how care professionals deal with situations, where language barriers are and what strategies use them successfully to overcome language barriers - in the best case -. On the basis of the insights gained are outlined and explained the need for action and support services can use it for critical situations as well as continuing education opportunities.
