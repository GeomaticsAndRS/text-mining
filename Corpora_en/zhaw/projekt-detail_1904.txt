In the field of sustainable development exist worldwide numerous indicator systems which to measure this complex process and promote. Nevertheless, there are unfortunately hardly any noteworthy progress to watch. In this EU-research project therefore the question tries to answer how such indicator systems can be implemented better in political and social decision-making processes. The project teams comprise this transdisciplinary from four countries representatives of universities and municipalities. Also a practitioners Guide (this also on German) was published as a result in addition to the research report.
