College for special education the aim of the workshop is to enable a framework for cooperation for the transfer of the CEFR (dtsch. GER) to create sign languages. The CEFR is a European reference system for the classification of foreign language skills at different levels (A1 to C2) that captures so far only spoken languages. The ESF workshop brought together experts on European level, the national developments to clarify, be replaced on the next steps, to link the ongoing and planned projects and to launch a common European development project. (Completion: September 2011)
