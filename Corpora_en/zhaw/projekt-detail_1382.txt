The project RISK - research and information management for language and communication is a synthesis and further development of the already existing teaching and learning of the course of research, as well as the E-learning courses developed at the IUED tools for translators and Internet per mediatori linguistici. The RISK Portal provides the corresponding content prepared and optimized for blended learning, and all those (diploma students, lecturers, students and language professionals) available who have the need to look up the various resources, search tools and search options or to deepen.
