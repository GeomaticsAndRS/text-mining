The so-called expert knowledge is increasing enormously, the demand for information grows, but can't often transferring this knowledge into a form understandable to non-specialists. The mentioned knowledge gap manifests itself in most areas of research, especially in technology. Many engineers act as?Experts? and writing technical texts. The writing skills of engineers based often only on general training in grammar and stylistics. For the transfer of knowledge between technical experts and laymen, it takes but differentiated communicative and cognitive skills and tools that are based on scientific concepts (from reading and understanding research, cognitive science, etc.) as well as can be practically used in everyday writing practice of NichtlinguistInnen. I students in the classroom with K in the 5th semester instruments were developed, evaluated and modified. In a larger follow-up project - in cooperation with business partners - write IT experts to transfer knowledge to be investigated and optimized.
