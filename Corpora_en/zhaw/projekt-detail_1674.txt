On behalf of and in cooperation with SiemensVDO (D) developed the IMS in the framework of the project «Park mate» the next generation of parking AIDS. Park mate is a system for semi-automatic parking, which missed the parking space and calculates an optimal trajectory to the parking. The steering is automated during the operation of the Park and the driver must only brake, turn, accelerate. An HMI (human machine interface), of particular importance to the interacts between the mechatronic system for parking and the driver (which retains responsibility for the vehicle).
