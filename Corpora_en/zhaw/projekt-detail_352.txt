The first two cohorts of graduates and graduates of the master's programme in social work took part in the I of course evaluation project in the spring of 2013 and 2014. In a period of two months each, the graduating FS2010 were online using standardized questionnaire and retrospective to her master studies, questioned their core skills as well as her career after training to FS2013. The aim of the study is to explore practical relevance and opportunities for development of the course from the perspective of graduates and graduates.
