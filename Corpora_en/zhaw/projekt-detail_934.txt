IRC solutions AG - the risk compliance experts value-at-risk (VaR) as a measure of risk has become global financial institutions of all kinds. The measure originated in the banking industry, because correct specification for all (market) risk in a number is expressed, can be compared directly with the existing own resources (risk buffer). This immediate economic interpretation of the risk measure VaR helped the worldwide breakthrough. Not only banks, insurance companies, asset managers, hedge fund managers and other financial institutions have recognized its benefits, but also the regulation authorities of banks and insurance companies. VaR risk measure has enshrined also in the Swiss legislation. In the Bank Act and its regulations, as well as in the collective investment schemes Act and its regulations required by professional actors, that the risk with the VaR risk measure is quantified.
