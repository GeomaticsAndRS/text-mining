Plants as a means of therapeutic nursing interventions plants play an important role in the lives of many older people. With its entry into the nursing home, they lose the ability to take care of their plants but often. Two nursing homes in the canton of Zurich study the possibility to integrate Interior plants in the everyday life of residents and residents together with the ZHAW. The aim of the project is there, the quality of life, increasing the attractiveness but also the quality of care of the elderly. For this purpose, the Institute develop for environment and natural resources and the Institute of nursing in an interdisciplinary project together appropriate nursing interventions and a matching Interior green. This use of plants as a means of therapeutic nursing interventions is realized within the framework of the CTI (Commission for technology and innovation of federal) project "Integrative Indoorbepflanzung of age centres in combination with plant-based health - and nursing".
