The professional initial training powerstation is a semester of motivation for young people who have completed secondary school C (or G) in the summer of 2005. Through the promotion of social and vocational skills, graduates and leavers should be prepared on vocational training and everyday work. Target is the vocational and social integration of these young people. The HSSAZ evaluated the professional initial training on behalf of Zurich in seam. The study focuses in particular on the detection of possible changes in the field of socially competent behavior. The evaluation includes an observation of the young people in a group discussion at the beginning and at the end of the semester of motivation as well as a short survey of jobs. Project end: 30.9.2007
