Establishing an ecological balance sheet database in the field of agricultural and food products is the sustainability assessment of and optimization of products and associated processes. For this create the basis in primary production the two research groups, geography of food and renewable energies of the IUNR by means of joint research projects in cooperation with companies or on behalf of research-promoting institutions. In cooperation with the Department sustainability and energy of the ILGI be created eco accounts in food processing and integrated into the database.
