Increasingly make Swiss SME direct investment in Eastern European countries. The deal is the biggest hurdle with differences in mentality. The research in the field of international business is able to offer the company until today no help to overcome these hurdles. The aim of the project is to identify the relevant cultural differences between the Switzerland and Eastern European target markets and to develop best practices in dealing with these. (The ZHAW is with university partners, the lead has the HTW Chur)
