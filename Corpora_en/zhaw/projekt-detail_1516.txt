Pitched roof monolith is a subproject of the innovation project pitched roof. In contemporary buildings with monolithic expression, the formal distinction between roof and wall is often short. Rather, roof and wall surfaces form a continuous, comprehensive all buildings cover. At the Centre of structural design ZKE examines this circumstance in terms of a constructive means. The interest is construction systems that are independent uniformly find use of location and position. This topic was in the frame of the option compartment constructive researches (master's course architecture) edited by students during a semester. Findings and results are currently processed on the ZKE and deepened. A report on this work will be published in the spring of 2007.
