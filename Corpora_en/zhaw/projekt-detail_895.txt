: 01.07.2010 partners / project partner (s): World Federation of science journalists research project examines whether a training project in science journalism in Africa and the Arab world met its objectives in terms of quality of reporting and social effects. To be extensive basic studies (survey of participating journalists, examination of the structures of the media houses, content analysis of the reports) undertaken. The project assesses whether a large scale training programs on science journalism in Africa and the Arab World achieves its goals. It aims at improving quality of reporting and achieving impact on policy change. Baseline studies and follow-ups on a large scale are conducted (survey of training participants, editorial structures in media houses, content analysis)
