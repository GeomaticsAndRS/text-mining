In the Yeast fermentation and production of bioethanol from sugar beet (beteraves) or potatoes, dregs incurred after the distillation of alcohol, containing also the yeast biomass in addition to actionable bad ingredients. These dregs have an energy potential, which can be exploited by anaerobic fermentation of organic gas. According to the bio gas fermentation, the Digestate in liquid form or as a separate solid represents a nutrient-rich organic fertilizer.
