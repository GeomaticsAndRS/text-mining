In the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), there were in the first parliamentary and presidential elections after the war end July 2006. The station set up by the Swiss Fondation Hirondelle Radio Okapi and French foreign station Radio France International are the most listened radio in this country. Radio Okapi has the explicit claim to be professional as well as the peace-oriented now. On the basis of a limited event (announcement of the results of the first round of voting in August 2006 and concurrent violent clash between militias) the reporting of the two radio channels will examine content affiliated Okapi and RFI are different radio and, if so, on what points exactly.
