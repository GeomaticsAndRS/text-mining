The Institute of mechatronic systems IMS is together with industrial partners Neuweiler AG Kreuzlingen and Tuchschmid AG to tranship Frauenfeld in a CTI project because, to develop a system, the fully automatic standard containers between trucks and railcars. A manipulator with 14 servo drives was built for this purpose. It is able to move the standard container under the contact wire of railway. The project has been successfully completed at the end of 2003 and aspects of it to the IEEE Conference intelligent transportation system ITSC 2004, October 2004, Washington DC published.
