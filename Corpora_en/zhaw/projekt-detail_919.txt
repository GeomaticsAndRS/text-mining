The part of the project "Open behavior in the pitched roof" is a further examination of the research focus "Pitched roof and increase" at the Centre of structural design ZKE. Authorities and the industry partner involved Eternit, VELUX and ZZ Wancor in addition to the College. The project aims at obtaining qualitative and quantitative findings as a decision-making aid for dealing with the sloping roof and the increase in inner-city areas. The main focus is on the open behavior respectively on the relationship of inside and outer appearance.
