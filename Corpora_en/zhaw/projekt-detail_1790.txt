The aim of the project is the development of detection methods for B-toxins such as ricin, Botulinumtoxins and Staphylococcus s. The work focuses mainly on the establishment of immunoassays. In cooperation with the research partners of Spiez Laboratory-specific antibodies are produced and purified. These are then in different Assayformaten including classic Elisa (enzyme linked immunosorbent assay) used.
