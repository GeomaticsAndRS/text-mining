Libraries benchmarking has aimed to create a continuous basis by means of an annual evaluation of appropriate indicators for comparisons between the currently 20 participating libraries. Because the participating libraries differ significantly in their main features, the basis for comparison is homogenized, by the libraries will be divided into four groups (scientific libraries, study and education libraries, regional and city libraries and libraries with Filialnetzen). Other types of comparison groups are possible. Through the regular collection of data long-term intertemporal developments should can be presented and developed standards.
