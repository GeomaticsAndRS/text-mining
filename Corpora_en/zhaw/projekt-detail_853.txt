WWF Switzerland for each municipality in the Switzerland the following data were obtained: – the current energy consumption – current energy production - the potential energy efficiency (through technical measures or changes in behaviour) - the potential for additional production of energy from renewable resources (solar, wind power, timber, hydropower, etc.) - the current and the expected recoverable self-sufficiency in the community with energy - impact of increasing the energy self-sufficiency of regional value added and thus on jobs in the region.
