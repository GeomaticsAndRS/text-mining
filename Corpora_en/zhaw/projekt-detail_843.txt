The Thurgau farmers is the weekly trade and Association organ of the Association of Thurgau agriculture (VTL). The VTL commissioned the IAM Institute of applied media science with the analysis of the substantive quality of the product and the development of scenarios for the relaunch of this publication. Project management: Dr. Peter Santos Herlach, head of consultancy IAM. Project collaboration: Colette Schneider, lic. phil.
