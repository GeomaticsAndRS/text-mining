Companies, institutions and organizations must constantly edit their reputation, their reputation, this is done for partners, clients as well as for (potential) employees. Discursive units, texts and text parts, used, which advertise not explicitly for products or for the company, but which indirectly contribute to the reputation, are to strengthen them or increase. In the context of this research project should be examines the linguistic implementation of reputation management on the basis of selected companies and linguistic expertise made available, how to transfer such strong cultural and language-dependent text in a multilingual context and translate.
