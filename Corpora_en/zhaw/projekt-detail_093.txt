Marketing and corporate communications of AXA Winterthur develop a digital content along with the IAM (research focus on organizational communication and public) hub with a company-newsroom. It's about the networking of different communication structures, processes, and products with the means of medienkonververgenten corporate public storytelling. The partnership includes work in the areas of analysis, modeling, testing, and organizational development. AXA Winterthur employs 4000 people and maintains a distribution network with 277 independent General agencies and agencies for a wide range of people -, thing - and liability insurance.
