Alliance Santé the Federal popular initiative "for a public health insurance scheme" calls for the creation of a national public institution for the implementation of the social health insurance. Such system switching of the today's competitive organized more POS system to a national health insurance unit is linked to costs. These costs have been quantified so far still not detailed. The WIG was commissioned a study, which is to close this gap and show a realistic estimate of the costs for the system exchange. Commissioned the study has given the alliance Santé.
