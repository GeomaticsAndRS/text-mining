For the previous old-age and nursing home Tgasa Missghettobaby in Curaglia in the municipality of MEDEL/Lucmagn, a new use is sought. One idea is the creation of a tourist service center. The research group tourism and sustainable development cleared out the feasibility of such a project in a business plan and pointed out ways for the necessary regional networking of tourist service centre in the Tgasa Missghettobaby.
