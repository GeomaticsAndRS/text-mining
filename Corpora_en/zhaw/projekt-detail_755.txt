Through the Zurich Foundation for occupational therapy funded project "Evaluation of occupational therapy treatment of patients with depression in relation to their unbearable" determine, in cooperation with the occupational therapy of the psychiatric University Hospital Zurich depressed diseased leadership skills related to their unbearable, as well as the action potentials of occupational therapy treatment. Occupational therapy offers a substantial and original contribution for the therapy objective of improving the unbearable. The research design is applied in the sense of the triangulation here more perspective and multi-methodical (combination of standardized survey, qualitative interviews and document analysis of foreign observation instrument). The sample includes 10 patients and the project extends over a period of 1.5 years. The project will contribute directly to the support of the profession in the form of a professionalization contribution through the impact analysis of occupational therapy.
