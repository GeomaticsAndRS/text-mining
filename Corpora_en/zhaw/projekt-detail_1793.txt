The company tally Weijl relied until now for the calculation of the number of items to be procured in the next year a particular dress family on the appropriate means of the sales figures of the previous period and the subjective assessment of the market of the product manager. The work aimed at the refinement of the forecast of the sales figures of the sales in the past considering the season, sale price, pulling discount and delivery bottlenecks. The method of generalized additive models (GAM) was used for determining the functional dependencies and visualization, with the gamma distribution with the log link function best to model the error was. The forecast of the sales was followed by generalized linear models (GLM). Despite these improvements the good intuition of the product manager will be still demand, which assesses the future fashion trends. Fashion trends are obviously not in the data of the past to find yourself.
