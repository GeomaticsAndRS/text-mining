Bureau of labour and social policy studies BASS for the health professions occupational therapy, physiotherapy, and midwives to researched data on the current state of employment and analyzes it to derive plausible estimates of the future needs on the labour market. The focus of the study is limited by the German Switzerland complexity reasons. In addition to the demand on the labour market to forecast demand for FH health study in the caring professions. In a first step expert-based, qualitative scenarios be developed to the demand future (by 2025) and the demand for training places in the German Switzerland for the mentioned health care professionals. Then quantitative forecasting models are calculated on the basis of these scenarios and statistical data.
