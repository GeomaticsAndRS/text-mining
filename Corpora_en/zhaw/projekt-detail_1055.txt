Duration: 2009-2010 This pilot project examined in detail the role of English when it is used as a lingua franca in higher education. The aim of the project was to outline a research agenda for future investigations into the topic and to formulate target areas for systematic language planning in higher education. Two studies have been published: Our research has been reviewed by the media: Sonntag (no 29, 25 July 2010: 4); NZZ am Sonntag (26 September 2010: 64).
