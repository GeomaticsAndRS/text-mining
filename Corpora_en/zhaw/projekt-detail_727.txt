The school of Engineering (SoE) as one of the leading technical universities in the Switzerland opts for future issues. In the context of the review of the organisational strategy, the SoE IAM commissioned Institute for applied media science with the analysis of the communication potential, of the media discourse and knowledge and the setting of the stakeholders. The results of these analyses have been evaluated in the context of strategy workshops and formed the basis for the development of the positioning and the communication strategy of the SoE. Project management: Markus Nagar, Manager training kit. Project team: Nicole Rosenberg, Thomas Gantenbein.
