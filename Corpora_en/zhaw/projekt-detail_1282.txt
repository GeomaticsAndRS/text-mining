A market basket analysis (WKA) for the electrical appliances that are settled through the SENS system performs the SENS, to calculate the disposal fees. This is a part of pending deliveries at the 12 largest recyclers of SENS branched off and sorted into two separate sorting systems for device categories and weighed/counted. The sorting is done in industry categories such as small household appliances, construction gardening hobby, lights etc., and foreign goods. The aim of the project is to analyze the current sampling strategy statistical methods and to optimize. In particular, it will be examined whether there are systematic errors in the current strategy.
