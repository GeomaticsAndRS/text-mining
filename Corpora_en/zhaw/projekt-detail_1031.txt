Persons with a migration background make up approximately 30% of the resident population of Switzerland. The group is very heterogeneous. However, should consist in equal opportunities for all people to be healthy and to the potential of their own health. The contract includes the design, implementation and analysis of a health survey in persons with a migration background. It is expected to generate knowledge about: - the health differences between the indigenous population and persons with a migration background in the Switzerland - investigated the health differences within the migrant population in the Switzerland - the most important determinants of educated health inequalities.
