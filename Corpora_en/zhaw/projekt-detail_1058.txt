IDP Institute for data analysis and process design the main task of this project is to accompany the pilot project of establishing a KoLe statistics (performance data of the health insurers) from a health economic perspective and advice. The main objective is to design the KoLe statistics as an important public health and health economic analysis tool and realize. This involves questions of effectiveness, appropriateness, efficiency (KVG art. 32) but also to questions of health care. -Database system: bring a health economic aspects of the actual / target analysis data-warehousing - statistical data analysis: bringing health economic aspects/target analysis statistical data analysis - development practice-relevant case studies, taking into account health-economic aspects
