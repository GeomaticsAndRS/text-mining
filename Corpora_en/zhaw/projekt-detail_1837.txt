Pro Infirmis Switzerland newly introduced the social counselling for people with mental disabilities in the summer of 2001 in seven cantons. In the context of a network of five universities for social work of the German and the French Switzerland, this pilot project is now scientifically and evaluated. The school of social work of Zurich in this scientific cooperation, conducting a client survey and developed an extrapolation of the Swiss demand for this new service. Together with the universities of Solothurn and Fribourg a so-called interface analysis is carried further, which will give you information on the acceptance and anchoring this new offer in the relevant environment. The project was completed in December 2003.
