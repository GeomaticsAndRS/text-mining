Subject of the project is an empirical investigation of the current internal and external communications practice at music schools. The study aimed to evaluate the various activities in the field of public relations, to identify the problems and needs of the PR responsible, to clarify the relationships with the media and provide information on potential opportunities for improvement. Method: written survey (300 music school head) / qualitative interviews principal: Herzka GmbH, Institute for education and counseling, Liestal
