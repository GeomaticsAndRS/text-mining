The sponsorship and the management of the children's home paradise in Mettmenstetten strive to align the home offer demand. This will be checked the previous offer that consists of two residential groups for children and young people, as well as in studios for the guided living, and clarified the possible need for new uses. Absorbed in the context of this needs analysis, as key persons (representatives of the mediating / admitting instances, competitors on the provider side, etc.) the supply situation in the region, assess the competition conditions among the providers and the development of adolescent and young adult problems. On this basis a selection will be worked out together with the sponsorship and the home management options, again will be discussed in a second wave with a selected number of key people and evaluated. The study is supplemented by a quantitative-oriented background analysis to clarify the market potential of the favorite options. Project end: 31 July 2007
