University St. Gallen, IMP the professorship in organizational communication and public of the IAM Institute of applied media science (Prof. Dr. Peter Santos Herlach) goes with the Institute for systemic management and public governance of the University of St. Gallen (IMP-HSG) (direction: Prof. Dr. Johannes Rüegg-Stürm) a project partnership. The aim is the research-oriented positioning of a training profile for management in challenging organizational and social contexts (HSG-master of management, organization and culture). A design of the strategic communication is being developed for one of current approaches of management theory, organization theory and management education-oriented cultural studies.
