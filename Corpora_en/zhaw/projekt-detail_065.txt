It performs a broad analytical survey of heavy metal content in defined Mono batches of green waste and material from the lawn maintenance and landscape conservation in the Switzerland (120 samples). The survey includes all heavy metals referred to in the ChemRRV, for which in recycled fertilizers limits exist (Pb, CD, Cu, NI, Hg, Zn). There are both regional and seasonal (time series) fluctuations of raw material into account, also is respected on a high diversity of the origin of the individual batches.
