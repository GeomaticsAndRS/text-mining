Deutsche Welle TV In the context of the wave TV is supposed to effect research and the evaluation of the German international broadcaster of Germans creates a structural and content analysis of selected news and magazine programmes by Deutsche Welle TV. In the English-speaking news and magazine articles are compared with the corresponding offers of competing stations such as BBC World, Al Jazeera, and France 24. The analysis will show you how the DW offer in terms of structure and journalistic quality differs from competing stations.
