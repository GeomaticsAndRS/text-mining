The literacy is a key qualification for the communicative tasks in our society and therefore an important basic skill by graduates, who daily have to do in their profession with different texts. The aim of the present project is the launch of the discussion on standards of education at tertiary level in addition to the survey of the reading skills of college students. Because both German and English texts occupy an important role in the professional life of students, is working with two languages. The relationship between reading literacy in the first language and a foreign language also belong to the research questions. The already developed, industry-specific reading skills test went through a pilot phase, in which he was tested on 700 business students in the fall of 2007. On the basis of the results of the pilot phase, the test is developed with the aim to use a standardized variant wide acquisition of literacy at the tertiary level.
