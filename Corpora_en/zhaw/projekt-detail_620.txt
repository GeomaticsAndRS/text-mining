The strategic management in the canton of Aargau in Switzerland via the development of rolling a ten-year landscaped development mission statement (ELB). 2012, the State Chancellery Aargau the IVM entrusted with a preliminary study for a perspective analysis. This study used mental models and instruments of the trend and future research and adapted them for the specific tasks and challenges of management. The recommendations drawn up with employees of the cantonal administration and external experts about the future significant strategic fields of action were then passed the Government Council. The development vision 2013 2022 of the canton Aargau is published on the Internet portal of the canton.
