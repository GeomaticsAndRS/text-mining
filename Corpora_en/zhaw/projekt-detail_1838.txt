Aim is the first website of the ZHW (1998 developed mid 99 switched on) to completely design and to apply new technical and design online opportunities. The new home page has 3 main functions: information platform for external, business card of the ZHW (PR - and marketing function), transaction portal for special user groups (project partners, training course participants, e-learning). Coarse detail concept were developed in the project team and for the graphical concept involved an external agency. The project core team designed and developed four databases that allow the ZHW members to showcase their expertise and services: personal profile with training and teaching activities, projects, publications, offers in terms of post diploma. The information from these databases are dynamically linked to the user. In addition, extranet also actual communication platforms are established for certain user groups (E.g. external students). The new homepage was switched on in the autumn of 2002.
