On behalf of the network health economics Winterthur, the internationally existing methods of systematic guideline development are analyzed within the framework of this preliminary project. In a further step, the need for a guideline is charged for the treatment of anterior cruciate ligament rupture and researched what international guidelines on this subject exist. As a result, various surveys, as well as a concept for the development of a guideline for the treatment of anterior cruciate ligament rupture in the Switzerland result.
