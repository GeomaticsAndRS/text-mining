Club Beverin nature park on behalf of the Association is Beverin nature park worked out a concept for a coherent, modular education programmes. The objective is the awareness by guests and locals for the diversity of nature and culture in the Park area as well as the promotion of sustainable regional development. The concept is tailored to the strategic objectives of the RNP Beverin and the potentials of the region. It picks up on the current scientific debate in the field of environmental education/education for sustainable development and derives modern didactic approaches. Keywords: environmental education, education for sustainable development of GNI, lifelong learning, learning region, educational concept, instructional design
