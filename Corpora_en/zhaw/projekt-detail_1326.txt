The mercy image stringing at bakery has been known since 1885 and is caused by bacteria such as Bacillus subtilis. This bacterium is both ubiquitous in the environment, is used in cleaning and Pflanzehbehandlungsmitteln but also as "Active ingredient". The mercy image is stringing a sweet fruit smell and visible mucous threads at the break up of the bread characterized. The Bacillus spp. or their spores can be entered with the flour in the dough. The spores are through the baking process to the stimulated germinate and can spoil the bread at wet warm storage eventually. Unleavened wheat bread are especially affected. Stringing can lead to huge financial losses annually from the industry's perspective, not to mention is also the loss of image of an affected producers.
