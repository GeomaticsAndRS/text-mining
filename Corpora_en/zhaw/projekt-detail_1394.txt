Swissport International Ltd. As a result of long-term trends, as well as random, daily, weekly and seasonal fluctuations, the incurred expenses at check in the airline business varies greatly. An accurate knowledge of the volume expected is central to planning, resource planning and budgeting. For this reason, a prediction model for total 5 cost and revenue drivers with a horizon of 6-18 months was created within the framework of this project. The methods used allow not only the prediction for the next budget period, but also the assessment of the accuracy of the forecast. The prediction model on the customers in the use and there leads to a better, causal understanding of the variations in the factors of production. Further, the results serve as a basis for discussion for management decisions in the budgeting process.
