Evaluation for the Department of education the school journal of the canton of Zurich is the cantonal publication of the Department of education for teachers and education authorities. After the school journal was launched in 2006 in terms of content and graphically new, IAM has commissioned the Department of education to evaluate use, evaluation and acceptance of new school Edition. After preliminary discussions with the editors and the editorial advisory group, a written survey is performed in March by teachers from elementary school, vocational school and high school.
