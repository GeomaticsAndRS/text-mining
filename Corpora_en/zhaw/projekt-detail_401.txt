The appraisal of guidelines for REsearch and evaluation (AGREE) is the international gold standard in the assessment of the methodological quality in the context of the development and application of medical guidelines. To implement and low-threshold application of AGREE, II in the German-speaking countries is necessary to the translation of the English original. The aim of the project is the German translation of the AGREE II instruments according to the method required by the trust AGREE. There is an agreement for the translation with the original authors of McMaster University, Canada. This is done in collaboration with the Institute for quality and efficiency in health care (IQWiG) Cologne and the Bern University of applied sciences for health.
