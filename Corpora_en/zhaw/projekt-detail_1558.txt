The aim of the project is to provide the instruments the employees of the client, which (inventory of health - and environmentally hazardous substances, hazardous building material inventory) offer the necessary Projektvorbereitungs and project completion (reporting) duties for a business process supported by MUMMY. The order for improved connecting the MUMMY technology to standard applications of the customer and therefore the internal recycling of MUMMY technology contracting authority. The HSW performs work in the field domain-specific software development and data integration within the framework of the job in the first place. Also, the developments under labour and business criteria are evaluated.
