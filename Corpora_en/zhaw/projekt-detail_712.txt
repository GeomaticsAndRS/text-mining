: 01.07.2011 partners / project partner (s): DSwiss AG the "data safe for professional users" provides in-house and corporate cross-team can be a easy to use and at the same time highly secure online platform available, with which documents securely managed and exchanged with each other and with third parties. This real need can be covered only very unsatisfactory with currently available products, because these are typically uncertain, limited with regard to the possibilities or user-unfriendly, making them unsuitable for less technically savvy users. This project aims to develop of an Internet-based service, to provide an easily usable and at the same time highly secure data safe with the most important functions to moderate cost any team. The existing DSwiss data safe technology with several modules must be expanded to require what some complex technical challenges offer and according to innovative solutions.
