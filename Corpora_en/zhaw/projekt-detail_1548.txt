Subject of the project is the study of the influence of forward-looking Office concepts to those affected and the associated costs. To the functions be examined by offices on four dimensions, which are connected with direct and indirect costs, namely the land management, the social climate, the exchange of information and the health. The dimensions of social climate, information exchange and health are connected with each other, as they relate to the communication, which affects the climate and the exchange of information, can act as a noise and thus as a stressor. The results should be exploited in a Monitoringinstrument for new Office concepts.
