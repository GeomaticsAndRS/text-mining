The TV audience is relevant, researched clean, attractive told and clean formulated information by the tagesschau. A the tagesschau editorial aims to avoid any linguistic abnormalities, which could interfere with the attention of the audience watching the broadcast and disappoint the expectations. Such a negative impact can as well be triggered if the grammatical errors such as through logically unclear references. The language quality project focused therefore not only the classical grammatical error, but also the optimisation potential in the areas of language, reasoning, text dramaturgy and storytelling. In the analysis and the presentation of the results, thus not only the detection of errors was promoted, but a wider awareness of the language and the deepened awareness of various aspects of the journalistic use of language.
