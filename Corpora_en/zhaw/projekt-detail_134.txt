Swissport International Ltd. Swissport International Ltd. maintains a large pool of technical equipment for the provision of ground services for airlines (pushback tractors, stairs, etc.) at its various locations. The portfolio of this technical assets generates substantial costs. In the project, a lean, but nevertheless systematic, and the state-of-the-art appropriate methodology was developed to operate the fleet management strategically and operationally best. The methodology to determine the optimum service life for each vehicle/equipment with respect to its State and new alternatives on the procurement market. On this basis, generated an investment plan for the entire fleet, and determines a prioritization of renewal projects. The decisions that are proposed as a result of the new methodology, are transparent and easily communicable. Through the improved management of assets the costs, without however compromising the technical performance.
