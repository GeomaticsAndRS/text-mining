Where does the wealth of Switzerland and how he can be, is much discussed. To capture the current knowledge and opinions about the origins and the future development of Swiss prosperity on a broad basis, the Center for marketing management in cooperation with the Institute for banking finance and experts has designed the study of Swiss prosperity between past and future. Future achievers (students) and current service provider (experts and managers from different sectors) have been questioned. While the opinions of today's and future achievers are compared.
