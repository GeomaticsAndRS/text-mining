The Forum Lehrerinnen-and teacher education Lenzburg dealt 2010 with the question of the impacts of educational institutions in the educational discourse. IAM Institute of applied media science has in a key note speech presents the results of an own media content and public storytelling analysis on the subject and an action-oriented conclusions from this. Project management: Dr. Peter Santos Herlach. Project assistance: Katharina Urbahn, Angelica Elsie.
