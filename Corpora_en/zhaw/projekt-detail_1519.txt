The success depends 21 processes of local agenda, how many local actors and stakeholders can be involved and included. Therefore, it needs moderators of such processes with good knowledge in the field of sustainable regional development and with necessary skills to the promotion of much party dialogue, and creative problem solving. Train to LA21 should contribute to the development of the necessary skills and awareness-raising among the actors of regional and local LA21 processes. To do this, training courses and other educational materials are developed and tested. They are the instruments for people professionally involved with local Agenda 21 processes. Thus, train to LA21 contributes to sustainable development at the local level. The trainings include both the necessary and the substantive aspect of sustainability and Agenda 21 process and method competencies in addition also the necessary knowledge of the local political system (actors, skills, decision-making) should be expanded.
