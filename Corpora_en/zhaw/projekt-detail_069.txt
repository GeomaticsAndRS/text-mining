It creates an overview of concepts, technologies and equipment for the enzymatic and microbiological treatment of household rubbish. The individual concepts are evaluated with respect to their material and energy efficiency and General strengths and weaknesses are pointed out. The applicability of individual procedures and concepts in the Switzerland is rated as views of the current Swiss waste management. Recommendations on the implementation of the enzymatic and microbiological treatment level research and level of household rubbish are there application in the Switzerland.
