www.staudensichtung.de the perennial sighting (www.stauden.de) working group is an independent panel of experts from 17 sighting locations in different regions of the Federal Republic of Germany, Austria and the Switzerland-based. The Working Group aims to plant perennials ranges under different soil conditions and various climatic conditions and check. The assessment of the use value of individual species and varieties in gardens and parks simplifies the selection of reliable thriving perennials the plant users and allows a clear and high-quality design of their assortments the perennial nurseries. The coordination of the Working Group of perennial sighting is the Hochschule Weihenstephan-Triesdorf. Chairman of the Working Group of perennial sighting is Prof. Dr. Bernd Hertle.
