Federal Office of communications (OFCOM) In February 2009 has the GmbH, founded by Vinzenz Wyss acknowledged the Federal Office of communications (OFCOM) media quality assessment (MQA) and thus approved for the evaluation of quality assurance systems in private radio and TV broadcasters. Guido Keel is Managing Director of the GmbH. The organisers are obliged to set up an editorial quality assurance system in the context of new concession and regularly evaluate this. MQA focus in evaluating primary assurance of editorial services, which are to provide according to the performance order of the organizers. OFCOM has approved that MQA Institute for applied media science works in the poll of employees of radio and TV stations with the IAM. The cooperation ends survey is a key element of the evaluation.
