In a pilot project, the Foundation of retirement flats in the city of Zurich (SAW) tried psycho-geriatric residential support for tenants in their homes since autumn 2005. Thus it should be old and older people with mental impairments, to live in a home. The Winterthur Institute for health economics makes a preliminary cost comparison analysis, staying stationary care to compare the Spitex housing assistance. The age Foundation takes over the costs, as well as a part of the Organization of this evaluation.
