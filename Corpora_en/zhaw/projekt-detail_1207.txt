Political communication at the general meeting of the Swiss Tourism Association in St. Gallen: on August 27, 2008, Dr. Peter Santos Herlach has, head of advisory and programme managed CAS political communication, a workshop with Association and tourism directors as well as communications and marketing leaders led. He presented the participants the most important trends of the political public (communication compass) and developed strategies for the tourism industry of Switzerland with them, so including text designs for argumentative policy stories. Over 600 members, including sponsoring organizations, companies and industry associations include the Swiss Tourism Federation.
