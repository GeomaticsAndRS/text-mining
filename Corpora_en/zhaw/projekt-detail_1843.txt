In the framework of the project an Excel ® was optimal-based tool for determining loading instead of quantities and order points, developed with a constant demand and tiered transport and purchase prices are accepted. Also, the limited stop ability of the products and the risk of loss of the customer are taken into account. For entering the required calculation basis is an Excel ® worksheet; the output of the calculation results is done in tables and graphics. The developed tool is suitable as a decision support tool for the purchase planning and can be used by buyers of RAHN AG without learning curve.
