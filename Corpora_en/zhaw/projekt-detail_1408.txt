Different nutritional supplements with herbal ingredients are to be developed in the framework of the project. These food supplements serve to maintain the functioning of the body and support. In the first phase of the evaluation will be positioned the slides into the existing product portfolio, determines possible competitions and selected suitable plants. The efficacy and safety is evidenced by database research and created a benefit-risk profile. The selected plants to be processed in an optimum formulation. This will require the production of aqueous extracts. These prototypes are then analysed with regard to quality and stability.
