The development of a traffic jam forecasts system for the Swiss national roads network is clarified for the Federal Roads Office FEDRO. The project contract includes the lighting of the existing systems and methods for traffic forecasts, a needs analysis, as well as the clarification of questions with regard to the data requirements and data availability for present and future systems. A system concept for a traffic forecasting tool for the Swiss national roads network will be further elaborated. The project is realized in cooperation with Rapp TRANS AG, Zurich/Basel. Customer is the Federal Roads Office FEDRO.
