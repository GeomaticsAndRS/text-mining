Cultural management network In the cultural sector is a media change, whose Auswirkung so far rather behave is discussed in culture management: the visitors or users of cultural offerings are networked via the «Web 2.0» digital and interact via social media such as weblogs, YouTube or Facebook. As a result, The customers exclude increasingly the institutions and companies for the networks. The aim of this study is to enrich the discussion on the usage and profit contribution from social media to empirically backed evidence. From September 2009 to February 2010 the ZHAW conducted an online survey School of management and law among 344 culture providers. Following questions were at the Centre: – – such as intensive culture provider in Germany, Austria and the Switzerland use social media? -What are the objectives pursued social media marketing, and can prove a profit contribution?
