TA Suisse provide a fairness opinion for the Centre for technology assessment TA-SWISS in the interdisciplinary project "energy from the Interior of the Earth: deep geothermal energy as an energy carrier of the future?." The opinion answered the most important current legal questions about the deep geothermal energy in the Switzerland and Europe, shows the future legislative developments. This is the development of competence between the Confederation and the cantons of special interest. Also clear instructions be created, aimed primarily at policymakers.
