Swiss Post International-Swiss Post International is the business customer magazine \ Competence\ evaluate by the Professor for organizational communication and public at the IAM. The magazine is published twice a year in four languages and seven land issues in a circulation of 8000 copies. This is the second corporate publishing review IAM for Swiss Post International. It includes a content analysis on the basis of the corporate publishing code IAM and a quantitative online reader survey in Germany, Sweden and the Netherlands. Among other things the implementation of IAM recommendations for the further development of the magazine from the first review and the perception of the magazine are checked by the readership.
