Age Foundation commissioned the age Foundation and in collaboration with Curaviva and the Spitex Association Switzerland, the TIG created a baseline study of the understanding and the level of integrated care and support in the German Switzerland. To capture the actual situation and to create a "typology of federated solutions" 500 Spitex organizations and approximately 1000 lines of nursing home will be interviewed in addition to a literature review and interviews with experts in the framework of a quantitative survey. The final report can be downloaded on the WIG Web site.
