In this project, which is currently in its second phase, a teaching aid is designed to commercial English (business English), which is tailored specifically to the needs of apprentices or prospective Merchants in the basic training. The teaching materials as well as in licensed editions in countries such as Finland, Italy, France and the Netherlands will be launched thanks to international cooperation with a Swiss British publisher. The teaching link-up impresses with its clear understanding structure and the authenticity of the communication situations. Linguistic tasks based on the needs of young trainees in the modern business world. A Web site is part of the timetable with its various additional materials. Task the ZHW team members was the advice in the design of the learning goals and tasks and critical reading against drafts.
