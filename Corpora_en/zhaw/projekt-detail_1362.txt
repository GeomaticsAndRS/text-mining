This project is a contribution of applied landscape research with an inter - and transdisciplinary approach in Glarus Süd. Landscape, landscape consciousness and their potential for the identity of the population and regional development was investigated on the example of this peripheral region 2011 merged into the municipality. Awareness of landscape was made visible with a quantitative survey of the population, described the landscape based on field surveys, literature and plan analysis. The overlay of the two fields of research findings about the importance of the landscape for the identity and the regional development can be derive and formulate concrete approaches for the future development of space. This study shows how landscape ideas can be included in the planning and serve as a basis for the future spatial development in the municipality of Glarus Süd.
