This project developed a structured methodology for asset management, allows to reach this target in practice. Our new approach allows the operators of the infrastructure networks, maintaining the substance of the networks while maintaining quality at a significantly lower cost than today. The power distribution system and the water supply are considered sectors. The asset-management methodology value driven asset management (VDAM) is based on a comprehensive technical and economic consideration and supports the asset manager in its strategic decisions. The methodology is implemented in a new software product and made available to the users of "ready-to-use". First applications show a long-term increased benefit potential of 20-30%.
