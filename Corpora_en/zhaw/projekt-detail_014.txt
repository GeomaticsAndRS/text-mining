Rheumatism League Zurich background: studies have shown that people with rheumatic diseases often suffer from severe sleep disorders. A good supply of, a bed tailored to their needs and mattress is even more important for these persons. The rheumatism League Zurich offers advice on sleep and beds supply for several years and would like to further improve the consultation process around sleeping and the supply of beds and put on an evidence-based Foundation. Objective: The objective of this study is to gain the effect of advice offered by the rheumatism League Zurich and the carried out interventions, such as for example the readjustment of beds rust, etc. to investigate replacing the mattress on the pain and sleep quality of the clients/students and knowledge. Also, the sustainability of the intervention should be evaluated and examines what impact improved sleep quality and reduced pain on quality of life and everyday activities of the clients will have.
