The relatively tall and poisonous to grazing animals bracken (Pteridium Corsican) increases the risk of fire and reduced the natürschützerische and agricultural quality of the destination slope used for many decades in the military. Effort and effect of cutting and tearing out measures are examined by the vegetation analysis of the Zurich office in a multi-year effort. Keywords: bracken, Pteridium Corsican, evaluation, problem plant
