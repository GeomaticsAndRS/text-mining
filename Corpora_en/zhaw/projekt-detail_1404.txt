Competition in the SME sector is increasingly dynamic--adapt products and services and the innovation cycles are becoming ever shorter. Participants at the 4th Zurich SMEs meeting learn how a concise and clear branding allows the company to prevail successfully in the new environment. Fire-management experts from the fields of science, business and market research give insight into the State of affairs and demonstrate that branding is today far more than the development of a brand name or logo. -&gt; SMEs 4. Zurich meeting, Thu, June 14, 2007, Aula, ZHW School of management, Winterthur
