On behalf of the primary school care Uster, the Department evaluated social work of the Zurich University of applied sciences (ZHAW) the school social work of the primary school in Uster. Four school social work end recorded their activity at the primary school in August 2011. The introduction phase is limited to three years and is completed with an evaluation. During the evaluation, evaluated the achievements of the school social work and carried out surveys among teachers (standardized survey) and key persons (telephone interviews). The project will start in February 2013 and will be completed in November 2013.
