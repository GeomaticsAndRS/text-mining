Communicate Swiss information Conference of public administrations (SIKOV) authorities on the youth pass? In a speech before the Conference of Swiss information public administrations (SIKOV) in Winterthur, Dr. Peter Santos Herlach, head of consultancy IAM and lecturer for political communication, has taken position on this issue. He pointed out the limits of communication between the authorities and young people and advocated to strengthen efforts for a lifelong political education. This is the most important State task to tackle the communication problems between authorities and youths. The Swiss information Conference of public administrations (SIKOV) is an Association of information officers and services out of Switzerland and the Principality of Liechtenstein.
