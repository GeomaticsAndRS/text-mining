The present study on the subject of "the Swiss insurance industry 2015" is carried out by the Centre for risk insurance of the Zurich University of applied sciences Zurich. Goal is to create a practical forecasting of developments of the Swiss insurance industry for years to come. The study summarizes reflected by a panel of experts and complemented with existing analyses - the opinions of important representatives of the Swiss insurance industry - in scenarios. This should give clues on the future of the Swiss insurance industry all managers responsible for the business development of insurers, the associations and institutions and support so that appropriate business decisions. At the same time is derived on the basis of the developed scenarios of target skills for youth training in the insurance sector. So can be created early on targeted training programmes and thus the long-term competitiveness of the Swiss insurance industry contribute to.
