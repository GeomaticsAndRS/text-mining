Writing is regarded as key skill in many professions, and requires specific skills. Profession-specific writing should be considered more in the teaching of vocational training, as well as the University of applied sciences. However, optimizations of the write with regard to occupation-specific educational content as well as any curricular adjustments require a precise knowledge of the specifics of professional text production process. In a joint project by ISBB, BBW and the Realschule and vocational education of the University of Zurich Institute should as a first step, occupation-specific text production process be examined by engineers and engineers and are checked in a second step, whether and if necessary what aspects of professional writing could be conveyed in the classroom and what curricular adjustments that would be necessary.
