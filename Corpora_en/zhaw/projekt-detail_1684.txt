Usually vehicle scheduling or scheduling and planning major maintenance (except operating requirements) be carried today in the field of rail transport parallel but basically separate from one another. While the vehicle management already computer-assisted runs depending on the company, the maintenance planning is mostly manual. Together with Molinari Consulting AG, Wiesendangen, the Institute for data analysis and process design (IDP) in several steps to develop a methodology and a tool that allows the operators of rail vehicle fleets to automate their maintenance and maintenance planning and to take into account these operational requirements and restrictions.
