Employees of a larger operation in the service sector can participate in a mental health checkup makora AG. At high loads in the areas of stress, burnout and depression, a suitable Internet-based treatment module offered to the affected employees. Here it is online-coaching (stress, Burnout) or online - Kurzeit psychotherapy (depression) by a specialist. In the context of this F E-project is the range developed by the makora, a company specialising in corporate health management, and scientifically evaluated by the ZHAW specialist centre health sciences. The research to check whether: • develop valid screen tools for Internet-based diagnostics allow; • who are effective problem-specific, Internet-based interventions for the employees, making to reduce of the stress of of symptom of and improve well-being; • that worth itself in terms of economic costs and benefits to operate Internet-based interventions.
