Problem statement, goals: In contrast to other age groups of IV new pensions in the Switzerland are increasing among persons aged between 18-29 years as before. Psychic-related pensions constitute the largest group. An earlier and more efficient action on the part of the involved actors assumes that the risk population, the main influence factors for an early spin-off throughout and the potentially helpful measures accurate and valid can be described, as is the case today. Methods: 1) in the preparation phase 2 focus groups are conducted with experts to the clarification of questions and analysis strategies; (2) quantitative documents (dossier-) analysis of 500 IV dossiers of the target group (layered, disproportionate sampling); (3) descriptive and statistical analyses; "case-control design" of cases with and without retirement; Sequence data analysis of gradients; Typology of gradients (growth mixture models).
