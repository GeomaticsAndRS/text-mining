Disability-related musculo-skeletal disorders and chronic pain causes enormous costs. With a collection of functional performance (EFL) therapists examine the work-related physical performance. The EFL is developed in this project. This standard for work postures and work-related physical performance tests in 300 patients with a good motivation collected in a cohort study. Then, the correlations of the observation criteria at the stress levels in the physical tests are calculated in a validation study. So, the importance of physical performance for the work can be determined. The project was successfully completed.
