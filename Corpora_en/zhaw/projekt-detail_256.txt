On behalf of the Executive of Obersiggenthal, the focus of research in organizational communication and public of the IAM has investigated authority communication of the Aargauer municipality. The focus was the strategy and the use of media in Exchange with citizens and intermediate organizations. Including qualitative interviews and focus groups were used. The project led to a research report of agency communication media (staff: Prof. Dr. Peter Santos Herlach, Carola etter gamble, Dr. Carmen Koch, Dominik Batz and free employees).
