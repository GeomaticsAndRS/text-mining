Climate Change and Environmental Policy We investigate how economic activities influence and, in turn, are influenced, by the natural environment and we formulate policy recommendations to balance economic use and long-term conservation of ecosystem functions and services efficiently. We investigate how economic activities influence and, in turn, are influenced, by the natural environment and we formulate policy recommendations to balance economic use and long-term conservation of ecosystem functions and services efficiently. The specific issues include: How can regulation induce directed technological change that is socially beneficial in the longterm? How can tax/subsidy schemes be designed and applied to foster the adoption of clean technologies? How can global refunding schemes be designed to produce an efficient global climate policy?
