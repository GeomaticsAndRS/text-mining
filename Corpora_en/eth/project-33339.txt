Comparison of different carbon budget allocation schemes Comparison of two negotiation instruments that have been proposed to reach an international climate agreement. Whereas Weitzman (2014) favours an internationally binding minimum carbon price, Bretschger (2013) uses equity principles to distribute the global carbon budget until 2050. This project surveys the current state of global carbon emissions and their determinants.
