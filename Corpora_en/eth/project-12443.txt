Drittmittel aus SNF Projekten: National Center of Competence in Research (NCCR), Mobile Information and Communication Systems, (MICS), Project: IP8 - System and Software Architecture. This project investigates software architectures as well as game scenarios for ad-hoc networks. A special point of attention is the infrastructure that is needed to support typical information systems applications: data-bases, location servers, transaction servers, etc. in a self-organized setting.
