MOUNTLAND 2: Prioritization for adaption to climate and Backcast-ing tolerable future states to socio-economic changes U match supply and demand for ecosystem services in mountainous areas. The primary goal of MOUNTLAND 2 is to provide management and policy options that support society to make choices to promote sustainable development of mountain ecosystems. The project will use a backcasting approach which combines envisioned future tolerable states with the current system boundaries
