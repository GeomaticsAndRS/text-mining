Comparison of Various Original and Indigenous Walnut Provenances (Juglansregia) and other Juglans Species Different provenances of Juglans regia from the original area of distribution in the middle east, India and Pakistan are tested for frost resistance, phenology traits and stem and wood quality. Original sources, not selected for their fruit properties, are tested with the hope of encouraging better growing habits in closed stands as well as better stem and wood quality.
