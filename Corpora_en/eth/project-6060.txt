Privatisation of public buildings Operating and maintenance of buildings of public authority are increasingly expensive, not to speak of the extensive administration of the many single objects. It can not be denied that in individual municipalities serious discussions are led about selling their public buildings to private owners or at least about outsourcing the operating of these buildings. With this background, the question sets itself up whether it is possible and reasonable to privatise public buildings. In this case, the public authority would occur as tenants and had no longer to occupy with the operating, the maintenance and the administration of the buildings. At the example of school buildings from two municipalities, the feasibility of this approach is checked
