Investigation of the Neurofunctional and Neurochemical Basis of Neurological and Neurodegenerative disorders by MRS, perfusion and fMRI The objective of this project is the investigation of the interdependency of neurochemical basis and neurofunction as well as the detection of compensation mechanisms in neurological and neurodegenerative disorder as migraine, CADASIL, mitochondrial cytopathies, PSP, CBD and parkinson.
