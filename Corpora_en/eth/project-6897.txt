Physical-chemical Foundations of the Structure and Dynamics of Chiral Molecules We investigate the fundamental aspects of chiral molecules as defined by the subject heading. The studies include high resolution spectroscopy, laser chemistry and ab initio calculations, including the effects of fundamental symmetry violations. Among the molecules studied experimentally we have halogenated hydrocarbons and sulfoxides among others. Reviews: M. Quack, Angewandte Chemie Intl. Ed. Engl. 28 (1989) 571, M.
