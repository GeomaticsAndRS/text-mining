Bibliography of Swiss Art / Bibliography of the Preservation of Historic Monuments and Sites In spring 1997 Vol. 19 (1995/96) of the Bibliography issues. It covers monographs and journal articles (swiss and foreign) concerning Swiss architecture, art and artists, exhibitions in Switzerland and abroad (as long as swiss artists participated). The second part lists literature (monographs and journal articles) on general aspects on the preservation of historic monuments and sites as well a special listing of congress proceedings on the subject. The Bibliography can be purchased (sFr. 70.-) at the Institute für Denkmalpflege, ETH Zentrum, CH-8092 Zürich
