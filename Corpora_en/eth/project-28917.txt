Mountain Invasion Research Project (MIREN) The Mountain Invasion Research Network (MIREN) investigates at a global scale plant invasions into mountains with a particular focus on 10 study areas: Sweden and Norway, US Pacific Northwest, Swiss Alps, Hawaii, Canary Islands, Kashmir, Chile, Australian Alps, South Africa. The Mountain Invasion Research Network (MIREN) investigates at a global scale plant invasions into mountains with a particular focus on 10 study areas: Abisko National Park (Sweden and Norway), Oregon and Yellowstone National Park (US Pacific Northwest), Swiss Alps, Hawaii, Canary Islands, Kashmir, Chile, Australian Alps, South Africa.
