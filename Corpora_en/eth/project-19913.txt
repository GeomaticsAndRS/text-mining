Linking Hydrothermal Alteration, Serpentinization and Fluid Fluxes to Biological Niches at the Knipovich Ridge (ESF Collaborative Research Project H2DEEP) This is a collaborative research project as part of the ESF EuroMARC Programme and is aimed at studying geodynamic and hydrothermal processes and their links to the deep, hydrogen-based biosphere along the Southern Knipovich Ridge, one of the most slowspreading segments of the global ridge system.
