InfectX The goal of InfectX is to identify the components of the human infectome for a set of important bacterial and viral pathogens and to develop new mathematical and computational methods with predictive power to reconstruct key signaling pathways controlling pathogen entry into human cells. SystemsX.ch RTD Project InfectX - Systems Biology of pathogen entry into human cells. PI: Prof. Christoph Dehio, University of Basel, Biozentrum.
