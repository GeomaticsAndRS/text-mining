Comparison of Changes in Landscape Structures Within Short Periods of Time in VariousSelected Areas In several villages of the canton of Baselland (Switzerland) changes in landscape structures within the last 10 years are investigated and mapped. The results are to be compared per se and with regard to the following question: What are the dimensions of change in a village with realized Güterzusammenlegung during the last 10 years compared to a village without Güterzusammenlegung ?
