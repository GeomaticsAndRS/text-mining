Journal peer review. It's reliability, fairness, and predictive validity. A case study on Angewandte Chemie The goal of our project is to research journal peer review from multiple perspectives and using multiple methods taking manuscript reviewing for the journal Angewandte Chemie as an example The goal of our proposed project is to research journal peer review from multiple perspectives (authors and reviewers) and using multiple methods (e.g., mail survey, content analyses of the reviewersS recommendations, bibliometrics). Taking manuscript reviewing for the journal Angewandte Chemie as an example, the research project examines the question of whether the main criticisms raised against the reliability, fairness, and predictive validity of peer review are justified.
