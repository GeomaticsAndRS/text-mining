Epidemic Diseases, Overcoming Poverty and Political Institutions In this project, we examine the interdependencies between epidemic diseases, human capital accumulation, inequality and growth. The main questions are: How large are the long-run economic costs of AIDS? How should optimal policy programs be designed to combat AIDS? How should programs to alleviate poverty be designed? How can land reforms help to overcome poverty? Which political institutions can help societies to escape from poverty traps?
