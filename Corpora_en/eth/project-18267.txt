mt EAST/update multilocational teamwork Educational Advanced Support Tools This will be the third and last session of mt_EAST project-series, started in 2001. Its main focus is to explore new forms of remote collaboration like workshops, seminars and oneday-tasks between students/assistants/tutors of various international universities as didactic teaching method. It is planned to include as well experts, companies of the building industries and building authorities in future remote collaborations. On the other side, the project targets a large diffusion of the remote collaboration as didactical course inside ETH Zürich. This project ends in 2008.
