Successional processes in near-natural forests A model belonging to the family of forest gap models is being developed to study successional processes in near-natural forests in various parts of the temperate zone, including several mountain regions (European Alps, US Cascades, Changbai Mts in NE China). More detailed information can be found under http://www.fowi.ethz.ch/pgw/ForClim_main.htmhttp://www.fowi.e Project status: started 1990, ongoing.
