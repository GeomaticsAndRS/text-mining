Globalization, Firm Training, RD and Remuneration of Managers In this project, we examine innovation and training incentives, productivity improvements and remuneration of managers and workers when firms compete at a local, regional or global level. In this project, we examine innovation and training incentives, productivity improvements and remuneration of managers and workers when firms compete at a local, regional or global level. Specific issues are: Under which circumstances do firms invest in general training? How does globalization affect training by firms and apprenticeship systems? How does globalization impact on the remuneration of managers? How do wages depend on the wealth and age of workers? How does globalization affect R&D locations and R&D intensity?
