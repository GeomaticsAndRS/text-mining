Occupational Health: VIL - Virtual Learning Environment of the Post Postgraduate Course Work + Health This project aims at the realisation of distance learning by electronic media for the post graduate study of work + health. This pluridisciplinary program which educates together occupational physicians and occupational hygienists is offered by ETH Zürich and the University of Lausanne. Distance learning will be offered as a complementary program to the scheduled courses in the classroom and the practical training. VIL combines self-paced study and computer supported cooperative work. The scientific aims of the project are to investigate the employment of distance learning with new technologies in the actual context. First, the goal is to test how to optimize the pedagogical and didactical effects by applying multimedia and the newest learning technology. Second, data are collected for qualitative and quantitative analyses of degree of learning effects and satisfaction.
