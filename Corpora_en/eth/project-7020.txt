The Clinton Administration and the SSwiss Case": How influential was the Jewish lobby? The Jewish lobby is a powerful foreign policy interest group in Washington. The purpose of this study is to analyze how effectively it managed to influence administration officials and congressional committees. How responsive was the Administration to the World Jewish Congress's demands to get restitution from Swiss banks and the Swiss government for holocaust victims? Were the reasons manly domestic or foreign? Research Period: 1993-1997. Geographical Area: United States of America, to a lesser degree: Switzerland, Israel. Discipline: Political Science. Research Areas: Policy Studies, factors influencing policy makers, Foreign Policy, Political Theory, International Relations. Setting: Master Thesis (Lizentiat) at the University of Zurich.
