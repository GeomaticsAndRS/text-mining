DIASTOR The Swiss research project for the digitization and restoration of archival film. The Swiss research project for the digitization and restoration of archival film. Supported by the Swiss Commission for Technology and Innovation (CTI). DIASTOR is a cooperation between academic research institutions and the Swiss film industry. It combines film-historical knowledge with restoration ethics, advanced research in IT and technological knowledge of the service providers and engineering companies to offer custom-tailored solutions with the aim to bridge the gap between analog film history and digital technology.
