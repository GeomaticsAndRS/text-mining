Venture 2008 Venture 2008 is an initiative of the ETH Zurich and McKinsey & Company, Switzerland. The Swiss-wide business plan competition supports young entrepreneurs in founding a company. Venture aims to put to use untapped innovation potential by encouraging young entrepreneurs to develop a business plan.
