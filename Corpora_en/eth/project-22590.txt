Sociology of Swiss risk politics This project differentiates and maps elite understandings of national insecurity in Switzerland. It indicates contending conceptions of public danger and it addresses the internal structuring of the professional security field itself. This project differentiates and maps elite understandings of contemporary insecurity. It indicates majority and minority positions, and it questions the internal structuring of the professional security field itself. The aim of this project is to demonstrate the multiplicity of often contending visions of national insecurity in Switzerland, and to show how institutional actors are unevenly positioned to negotiate such visions.
