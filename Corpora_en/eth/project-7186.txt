Landscape Changes in the Reuss Plain and in the Upper Levels ofthe Bünz Valley (Aargau, Switzerland) Since 1840 Changes in landscape in the Reuss plain and the upper levels of the Bünz valley (Aargau, Switzerland) since 1840 are quantified by analyses of maps. Some roots of the intense changes are analysed and shown in balance-sheets. The investigations are especially focussed on the consequences of the Reusstalmelioration .
