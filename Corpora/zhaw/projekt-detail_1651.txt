Das Projekt Narrative Space  ist Teil des NCCR (National Centre of Competence in Research) Challenges to Democracy in the 21st Century .  
One of the main purposes of the NCCR-project Challenges to Democracy in the 21st Century  is to develop strategies for the improvement of journalistic performance in political communication. This goal is pursued within the cross-sectional research unit in the project Quality of Journalism.  It aims to synthesize and apply the research results that can potentially contribute to the journalistic quality agenda. The project focuses on the results of the media research projects carried out in Modules 3 and 4 of the NCCR project. It is intended to design and formulate strategies to improve the journalistic performance of political communication.
