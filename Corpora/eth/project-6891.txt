  Molecular Kinetics and Spectroscopy.Kinetics of Fast Gas Reactions 

 We develop new experimental techniques for the investigation of fast gas reactions after IR-laser excitation, in particular reversible reactions by the gas phase IR-laser T-jump technique. Backgroud reading: S. Jans Bürli and M. Quack, Molekulare Thermodynamik und Kinetik, Vdf Zürich 1986.
