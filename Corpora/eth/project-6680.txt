  Projects in Statistical Data Analysis  

The Seminar für Statistik includes a group for consulting and for executing  smaller and larger projects in statistical data analysis. Some recent studies deal with the following topics: - Security of high speed trains against side winds: Feasability of a protection scheme based on wind forecasts - Predictability of heart activity from blood oxygen concentration - Forming regions of homogeneous health expenditures - Modelling concentrations of components of vehicle exhaust on the basis of short term speed changes - TV usage: Assessing the precision of estimated market share and rating figures Such projects form the basis for applied methodological research and development as well as considerations about data analysis in general.
