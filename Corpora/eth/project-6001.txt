  Planning Public Open Space - the Use of New Media and 3D-Visualizations 

 The project which is funded by the Federal Office for Professional Education and Technology is concentrating on a comparison of traditional and new media as means for informing the public. The proposed urban design competition for the Zürich-Leutschenbach area is used as a pilot study. The results will also be transferable to non-competition oriented planning procedures and the public information process in general
