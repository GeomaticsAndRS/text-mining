  The lakes in Flims and Laax: New insights into the age of the Flimser landslide  
Several lakes sit on top of the Flimser landslide masses. Limnogeologic investigations of these lakes (Crestasee, Caumasee, Lej Grond), in particular the age of the sediments, will provide new insights into the still disputed age of the Flimser Bergsturz.
