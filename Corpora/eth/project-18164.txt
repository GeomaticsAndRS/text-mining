  Application of Molecular Biology Methods in Modern Carbonate Microbial Environments Implications for the Geologic Record  
This project conducts an insitu genomic analysis of the microbial community involved in dolomite formation in Lagoa Vermelha, Brazil, as well as a comparative microbial study of this semitropical hypersaline coastal system with the dolomite-forming evaporative sabkha  environment in Abu Dhabi, U.A.E.
