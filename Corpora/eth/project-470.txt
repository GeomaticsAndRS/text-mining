  Nachhaltige Pflege empfindlicher Denkmäler (Sustained care of sensitive historical monuments. Methods of investigation on sensitive monuments as a tool for sustained care) (laufend)  
Wall paintings, painted stones and stuccoes have often undergone several restorations and are preserved in a fragile state. Damage can be avoided when the objects are kept  in appropriate environmental conditions which are elucidated by combininmg climatic monitoring and modelling
