  Vergleichsstudie zu verschiedenen Feuerbrand-Bekämpfungsstrategien: Vom Anbau zum Konsum  
We are comparing different fire blight control strategies such as the application of pesticides, biocontrol agents and streptomycin and the use of resistant cultivars obtained  by classical breeding or a Cis-genetic approach with respect to feasability and biosafety.
This project is a project initiated by the Swiss Expert Committee for Biosafety and financed by the FOEN
