The Health-IS Lab is a joint initiative of the University of St. Gallen and ETH Zurich that combines information technology and behavioral sciences to promote well-being and a healthy lifestyle. We build and evaluate consumer-centric health information systems that have not only the potential to improve outcomes and reduce costs of health interventions but also to support health promotion and disease prevention strategies of organizations.
