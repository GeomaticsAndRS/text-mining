  Phantasien, Fiktionen, Abstraktionen. Provisorisches Wissen in sozio-ökonomischen Verhältnissen ca. 1880-1930 / Phantasies, Fictions, Abstractions: Provisional Knowledge in Socioeconomic ˝ Relations ca. 1880U1930  
The project deals with the economic and social conditions of knowledge in the era of energeticist theories 1880-1930. It examines the economic rationale of the production of knowledge as well as the contemporary critique of its epistemic and social imbalances.
