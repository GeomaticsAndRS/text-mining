  HFN, a high-precision (128 bit) package for numerical computation on CELL Broadband Engines (IBM CELL Processors) 
The aim of this project, which involves students in CSE and physics from ETH Zurich and the University of Zurich, is the provision of a high-precision (128 bit) package for numerical computation on CELL Broadband Engines (IBM CELL Processors). Cooperation with NERSC and UC Berkeley.
