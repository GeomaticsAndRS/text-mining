  In preparation: Forestry Management and the rural population in the Western partof Madagascar (working title)  

 The dense and dry forests of Western Madagascar are expolited by the population of the villages (wood, non timber products, shifting cultivation) as well as wood in the field of forestry concessions. Important research results in ecology, sylviculture and wood exploitation are available. However, the interface mankind - forest and the needs of the population have not been considered in depth up until now. The aim of the project is the sustainable management and preservation of forestry resources for the advantage of the rural communities.
