  Drittmittel aus SNF Projekten: National Center of Competence in Research (NCCR), Mobile Information and Communication Systems, (MICS), Phase II, Cluster 4 - Project 4.5  
This project investigates serious game scenarios for mobile networks serving non-entertainment purposes such as educational training, policy making, or marketing. For our pervasive application prototypes, we are using technologies such as gesture recognition, geopositioning, and biofeedback.
