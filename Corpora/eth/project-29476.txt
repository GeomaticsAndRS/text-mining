  Glaciological investigations in the Mattmark area in connection with the danger of large ice falls from the tongue of Allalingletscher  
On 30.8.1965, 2 millions m3 of ice broke off from Allalingletscher and destroyed the Mattmark dam construction site killing 88 people. Since the completion of the dam the tongue of Allalingletscher is continuously monitored to be able to detect potential danger in time.
