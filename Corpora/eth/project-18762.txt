  Investigation of the Neurofunctional and Neurochemical Basis of Psychiatric Diseases by fMRI and MRS 
Patients with depression and schizophrenia suffer from a self-reference  disorder. Using ketamin as a drug model for schizophrenia in healthy volunteers as well as real patients the interdependence of neurofunction and neurochemical changes is investigated using fMRI, MRSI and 2D j-resolved MRS.
