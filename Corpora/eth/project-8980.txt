  The effects of beta-blockers, aspirin on procoagulant activity, expression of cellular adhesion molecules, and endothelial activation in response to acute mental stress: A randomized controlled trial  
The study investigates the effects of beta-blockers, aspirin and natural habituation on procoagulant activity, expression of cellular adhesion molecules, and endothelial activation in response to acute mental stress by means of a double-blind randomized controlled  trial in middle aged individuals.
