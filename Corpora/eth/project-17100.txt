  Micro Electromechanical Mirror for Tunable Resonant Cavity Enhanced Infrared Sensors for 2-5 um Wavelengths 
Development of an electrostatically actuated micro-electromechanical  mirror for a Tunable Resonant Cavity Enhanced Detector (RCED) in the near infrared domain. Fabrication includes standard microfabrication processes such as Photolithography, DRIE, PVD, chemical etching, wafer bonding, dicing.
