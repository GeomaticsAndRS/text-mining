  Preservation of Historic Parks and Gardens  

To encourage the preservation of historic parks and gardens, only hesitantly being adopted  in Switzerland, and as a contribution to the international discussion of principles, a research concentration in the preservation of historic parks and gardens was established. The present project includes: a) Preservation of Historic Parks and Gardens and Nature Conservation (Study project and Publication): Investigation of the causes of conflict between these two organizations and a research for a solution to the conflict, in collaboration with Prof. Dr. Ingo Kowarik (University, Hannover) and Prof. Dr. Erika Schmidt (Technical University, Dresden). b) Register of historic gardens in Switzerland: Scientific assistance to the project to be carried out by ICOMOS, the Swiss Heimatschutz, and the BSLA.
