  Direct-fed microbials with high anti-Salmonella activity for poultry and swine livestock production  
Direct-fed microbials containing bacteriocinogenic intestinal strains, Bifidobacterium thermophilum RBL67 (thermophilicin B67) in combination with Pediococcus acidilactici UVA1 (pediocin PA1) are developed to inhibit Salmonella in poultry and swine for livestock production, with mechanistic basis.
