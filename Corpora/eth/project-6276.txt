  Influence of a retailer to enhance resource efficiency in the food production chain  

 This research project aims at showing the most promising actions a retailer could take to improve the resource efficiency of the activity "to nourish". It is performed in collaboration with the leading Swiss food retailer Migros. At first, the material and energy fluxes of the food production chain are studied. Scenarios will then be developed to describe the possibilies of improvement in the system. An analysis of the money fluxes will complete the study.
