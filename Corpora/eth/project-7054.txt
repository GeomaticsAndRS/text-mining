  Galileo Galilei "Dialogo sui massimi Sistemi"(1632)  

We are preparing a critical edition based on the princeps of 1632. It shall  contain all the corrections and variants of the specimen Cart. 352 of the Biblioteca del Seminario in Padua and all the variants of the florentine original manuscript. Furthermore the commentary shall give evidence of the knowledge acquired by the author through the study of ancient and modern authors.
