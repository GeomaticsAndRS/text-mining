  MESEU - Mapping of ecosystems and their services in the EU and its member states  
The EU Biodivesity Strategy to 2020 requires member states to map ecosystem services under Target 2, Action 5. The implementation of Action 5 is supported by the MAES working group. PLUS (A. Grêt-Regamey) is a member of the consortium and contributes with expertise in mapping of ecosystem services.
