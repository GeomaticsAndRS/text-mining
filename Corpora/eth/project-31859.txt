  PROSET - Productivity Increase in the Context of Customer Interaction through Service Experience Management  
The project aims at developing a theoretically and empirically sound concept to measure, manage and design service experiences and thereby increase service productivity in the context of customer interactions. 
The project PROSET aims at exploring how to achieve an optimum of service productivity with respect to these conflicting goals of Marketing (benefit) and Operations Management (costs). In short: How can the competing goals of Marketing and Operations Management be aligned to enable optimized service productivity?
