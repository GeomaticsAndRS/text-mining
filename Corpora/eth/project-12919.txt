  Monitoring changes of Allalingletscher in connection with the danger of large ice falls from its tongue  
On 30.8.1965, 2 millions m3 of ice broke off from Allalingletscher and fall down towards the Mattmark dam construction site killing 88 people. After completion of the dam, the endangered area should be evacuated in time. For this reason the Allalingletscher tongue is continuously monitored.
