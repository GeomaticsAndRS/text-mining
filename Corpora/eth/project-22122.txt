  From design to industrial scale processing of food grade NAnoCOntainers for NUtrient encapsulation (NACONU) 
Food grade and amphiphilic block copolymers shall be designed and synthesized, allowing for the formation of nano size vesicle structures for encapsulation of functional components (FCs), in particular nutrients. For shaping / filling Vesicle Containers (V-Cons) processing technology will be develop
