  Experiences with Revision and Implementation of New Forest and Environmental Legislation in European Countries with Economies in Transition  

This international network examines present trends in adjusting legislation to new social, economic and political conditions. It provides an opportunity for researchers, government officials and forest owners to exchange experiences on implementing legislation addressing sustainable forestry development, forest conservation and nature protection in forests. The 3rd International Symposium will be organised in Jundola/Bulgaria, June 12-16, 2001.
