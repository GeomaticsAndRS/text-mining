Aussprachetraining Deutsch mit adaptiver mobiler App: multimediales, kursunabhängiges Lernen und Blended Learning (2015)

Projektleiter/in: Jörg Keller

Projektteam: Juan Carlos Diehlmann, Peter Eisenegger, Bobby Rohrkemper, Emilio Trigoso

Projektfinanzierung: KTI

Projektvolumen in SFr.: 330 000

Projektbeginn: 01.01.2015

ProjektpartnerInnen: Native GmBH und atfront AG

A person's accent and pronunciation are important aspects of their identity. Beyond the desire to be well understood, German learners also have deeply personal reasons for improving their phonetic skills.
We will build a mobile app that transforms successful in-classroom exercises to a blended learning approach that can also be used individually. It will teach and test both theoretical knowledge and practical skills including essential phonetic phenomena.
(Status = eingereicht, September 2014)
