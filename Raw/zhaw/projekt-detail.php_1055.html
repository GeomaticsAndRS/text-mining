EMI English-medium instruction in Higher Education (2009-2010)

Projektleiter/in: Patrick Studer

Projektteam: Annabarbara Ehrensperger, Paul Kelly

Projektfinanzierung: internal seed funding

Projektbeginn: 01.08.2009

Duration: 2009-2010
This pilot project examined in detail the role of English when it is used as a lingua franca in higher education. The aim of the project was to outline a research agenda for future investigations into the topic and to formulate target areas for systematic language planning in higher education.

Two studies have been published:
1. Mehrsprachigkeit an universitären Bildungsinstitutionen (http://pd.zhaw.ch/hop/1222889142.pdf);
2. Case analysis of a physics lecture in English (Bulletin VALS-ASLA 2010 at http://doc.rero.ch/lm.php?url=1000,20,10,bulletin_vals_asla_2010_special_1.pdf)

Our research has been reviewed by the media: Sonntag (no 29, 25 July 2010: 4); NZZ am Sonntag (26 September 2010: 64).
