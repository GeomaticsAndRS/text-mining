Persönliches Profil

Aus- und Fortbildung
1992 - 1997: PhD at the Physics Institute of the University of Zürich in the group of Prof. P. F. Meier. Title of PhD thesis: “Time Evolution of Quasiparticle and Phonon Distribution in Superconducting Films”
1991: Diploma in theoretical physics. Specialization in mathematical physics and theoretical particle physics. Title of the diploma thesis: “Quantenmechanik inkompressibler Systeme” (Prof. J. Fröhlich).
1986 - 1991: Study of physics at the ETH Zurich.
1985 - 1986: Work as a repairman at SIBIR AG.
1981 - 1985: Kantonsschule Aarau, Typus C.

Beruflicher Werdegang

2010 - present: Lecturer (physics) at the ZAMP (ZHAW)
2008 - present: Lecturer University of Zürich (“Lehrauftrag”).
2008 April 1: 75% Senior scientist at the AILab, 25% research fellow at ECLT
2007 October 1: 50% Senior scientist at the AILab, 50% group leader in BioMIP, Ruhr University Bochum
2007: Invited guest researcher at the ECLT in Venice, Italy.
2007: Co-PI in BioMIP part of the German systems biology project “Endosys”.
2005: Founding of ProtoStream GmbH.
2005: Co-PI in BioMIP part of the German systems biology project “Systemology”.
2004 - 2007: Group leader position in BioMIP, Ruhr University Bochum.
2001 - 2004: Group leader position in BioMIP at the Fraunhofer Gesellschaft (FhG) in St.Augustin / Bonn.
1999 - 2000: SNF grant. Guest scientist in the group of Prof. J. S. McCaskill at the IMB Jena and later at the Gesellschaft für Mathematik und Datenverarbeitung GMD in St. Augustin / Bonn.
