Green Roofs for a greener environment

Eu-Project, a Grundtvig learning partnership project

Projektleiter/in: Nathalie Baumann

Projektbeginn: 31.08.2011

Weiterführende Informationen: http://www.grundtviggreenroofs.com/

Transfer of knowledge and exchange from ZHAW Switzerland to Turkish and Spanish partners in order that they understand the principles of Green Roofs as one of the measures to improve the greening of city, the biodiversity of cities and the climate/health of cities. The output was to install one green roof in each partner countries and to develop a booklet to inform different stakeholders in each countries.
