Kick-Starting Projects African Countries

Improved Fish Nutrition in Uganda and Kenya

Projektleiter/in: Constanze Pietsch

Projektfinanzierung: Global Affairs, Universität Basel

Projektvolumen in SFr.: 18 000

Projektbeginn: 01.01.2015

ProjektpartnerInnen: Aanyu, Margaret, Aquaculture Research and Development Center (ARDC), Kampala, Uganda und Okoth, Sheila, School of Biological Sciences, University of Nairobi, Nairobi, Kenya

This cooperation aims to lower the costs of fish feeds by formulating fish feeds based only on plant ingredients, and at the same time to improve the quality of fry and productivity of adult fish through optimized fish nutrition by choosing adequate feed ingredients with lower mycotoxin contamination. The present project only includes the collections and analyses of samples of plant origin or from fish feed from Uganda and Kenya. The kick-start for this project requires exchange visits of Swiss and African researchers.
