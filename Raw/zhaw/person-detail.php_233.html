Arbeits- und Forschungsschwerpunkte, Spezialkenntnisse
Active Packaging: Development of active packaging technologies such as antimicrobial films, oxygen scavengers, moisture absorbers, ethylene absorbers etc. to preserve the quality, improve the safety and extent the shelf life of the packaged food:

Nanotechnology: Application of nanotechnology to improve the properties of packaging films

Shelf life Designed Packaging: Development of simulation program simulating the shelf-life of packaged food to optimize the packaging material, dimension and packaging process

New Packaging Materials: Development of packaging materials from renewable resources
