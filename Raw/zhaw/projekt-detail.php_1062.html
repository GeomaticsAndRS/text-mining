Translation processes

Untersuchung von Prozessen beim Übersetzen

Projektleiter/in: Beate Sick

Projektfinanzierung: DORE

Projektbeginn: 01.08.2009

In the Capturing Translation Processes (CTP) project1, we are monitoring
student and professional translators at various points in their careers. The data
allow comparisons between the same students at the beginning of their
translation degree program, the end, and 2 years post-graduation as junior
professionals as well as professionals with different levels of experience.
The statistical analysis is done at the IDP while Maureen Ehrensberger-Dow at Dept L has the lead of project.
