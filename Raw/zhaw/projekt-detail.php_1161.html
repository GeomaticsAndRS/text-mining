Modelling and simulation of phenomena in vaccum interrupters

Projektleiter/in: Peter Biller

Projektteam: Flavio De Lorenzi

Projektbeginn: 01.01.2009

ProjektpartnerInnen: ABB

Vacuum interrupters are key components in medium voltage applications and have the task to cut short-circuit and load currents. The underlying physics of metal vapour arcs is very complex. Modeling and simulation can serve to better understand the phenomena, identify the influence of various parameters and optimize the design for better performance.
