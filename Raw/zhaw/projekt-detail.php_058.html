ExpAct - Experiences keep people Active

ExpAct

Projektleiter/in: Andri Färber

Projektteam: Alexandre de Spindler, Eva Gmür, Theresa Hodapp, Irina Nast, Mandy Scheermesser

Projektfinanzierung: EU

Projektbeginn: 01.09.2014

ProjektpartnerInnen: European Academy of Bozen, Andrássy Universität Budapest, Ethical Software Soc. Coop, Aktivsenioren e.V, powerAge Foundation

The goal of the project is to improve the quality of life for older adults by strengthening their social inclusion, improving social interaction and self-esteem. The ExpAct project will develop ICT-solutions in conjunction with accompanying services to enable older adults to make full use of their potential – their experience. The aim is to get primary end-users engaged in professional work again or help them to become engaged in unpaid voluntary work as their skills and expertise are too valuable to be left unused.
