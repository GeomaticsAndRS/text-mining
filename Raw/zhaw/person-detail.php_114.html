Peter Meier is head of the Centre of Alternative Investments & Risk Management at Zurich University of Applied Sciences (ZHAW). He studied at University of St. Gallen where he received a doctoral degree in economics in 1982 and was a visiting scholar at Stanford University. At the Cantonal Bank of Zurich he was Chief Economist before he started to build up a modern asset management unit for institutional and private portfolios as well as mutual funds from 1992 to 1998 for the same bank. As CEO of Swissca Portfolio Management Ltd, Peter was responsible for one of the larger Swiss institutional asset management companies.

Beruflicher Werdegang / Professional milestones
Prof. Dr. Peter Meier ist Dozent an der Zürcher Hochschule in Winterthur. Seine Forschungs- und Lehrgebiete umfassen Investitionstheorie und Asset Management sowie Risikomanagement und Alternative Investitionen. Er leitet das Zentrum für Alternative Investitionen, das vom Transparency Council Funds of Hedge funds unterstützt und beraten wird.
Seine Promotion erlangte er 1982 an der Universität St. Gallen nach Studienjahren in Lausanne und an der Stanford University in Kalifornien. Bei der Zürcher Kantonalbank wirkte er von 1987 bis 1992 als Chefökonom und leitete anschliessend bei der gleichen Bank das Asset Management. Von 1998 bis 2001 war er Geschäftsleiter der Swissca Portfoliomanagement AG, welche Anlagefonds, Stiftungsvermögen für Pensionskassen und direkte Mandate für Vorsorgeeinrichtungen verwaltete.
Nebenamtlich wirkt er in Aufsichtsgremien für Vermögensverwalter und Pensionskassen. Seine Forschungs-. Publikations- und Vortragsschwerpunkte sind alternative Anlagen.


His research, speaking, consulting and teaching experience range from economics to finance and particularly banking, investment management, mutual funds and pension funds. Since 2000 he published regularly about alternative investments and hedge funds and is a speaker at international conferences. Peter Meier is also member of investment committees of pension plans and member of the boards of asset und fund managers. In 2002 he founded the Transparency Council Funds of Hedge Funds encompassing leading asset managers which support the ZHAW Centre to do research and to publish about funds of hedge funds. He is also initiator and cofounder of hedgeanalytics Ltd, a ZHAW spin out company which maintains www.hedgegate.com and rates funds of hedge funds.
