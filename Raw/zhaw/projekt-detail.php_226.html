Up on the roof!

EU-Projekt (Leonardo da Vinci, TOI)

Projektleiter/in: Nathalie Baumann

Projektbeginn: 01.12.2013

Weiterführende Informationen: http://www.biosolarroof.com

THE PRIMARY AIM
To build accredited training programmes that will develop workforce skills to install & maintain a green roof that will hold solar panels as well as stimulate pollinators.
OUR CONSORTIUM
A mix of small to large, public & private organisations united in developing innovative curriculum around sustainable rooftop construction. We offer higher education research, VET providers allied with private construction businesses successfully selling this service in the European marketplace.
OUTPUTS

To produce a programme of training for contractors on green roofs;

Designed to facilitate solar panels
Designed to stimulate pollinators
Designed to do both the above
OUCOMES

A unique & innovative training programme available to European contractors currently not offered elsewhere globally
New skills, new jobs facilitating competitiveness through innovation & knowledge
Further raise the awareness of green roof benefits
Stimulate enterprise & employment
