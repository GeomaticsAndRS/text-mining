﻿ Projekte ZHAW: Development of an in-situ sensor for fluorescent reporter molecules

Zürcher Hochschule für Angewandte Wissenschaften: Projekte

Development of an in-situ sensor for fluorescent reporter molecules

a contribution to PAT

Projektleiter/in: Bernhard Sonnleitner

Projektteam: Tobias Broger, Res Odermatt, Bernhard Sonnleitner, sonn sonnleitner, sonn sonnleitner

Projektfinanzierung: KTI

Projektvolumen in SFr.: 424 809

Projektbeginn: 01.03.2008

ProjektpartnerInnen: Aquasant AG

A commercially established in-situ sensor for determination of optical density is modfied such that fluorescence of biosuspensions can be measured. This sensor has been shown useful for the real-time quantification of fluorescent
proteins (GFP, BFP, YFP) and the autofluorescence of cells in bioreactors.
