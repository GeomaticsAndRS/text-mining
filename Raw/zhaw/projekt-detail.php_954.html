Set up your ERP with a questionnaire and without manual

Projektleiter/in: Gerold Baudinot

Projektteam: Stephan Bütikofer, Mathias Christen, Christoph Heitz, Marion Mürner, Karl Rege, Jörg Sigrist, Raimund Wüst

Projektfinanzierung: KTI

Projektbeginn: 01.03.2010

ProjektpartnerInnen: Posity AG

Posity ERP can be set up without reading a manual. The Posity solution incorporates a new revolutionary technology which allows the customer to configure his business processes by answering an online questionnaire. Further a visual business language maps the customer requirements and the system functionality.
Posity’s ERP solution provides a standard functionality which covers the requirements of most SAP users. This basic functionality can be implemented immediately and adapted easily whenever customer requirements change. Overall, companies will enjoy a cost reduction on services of factor 7 to 10 compared to other ERP systems.
