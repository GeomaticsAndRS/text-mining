OneXiO: bioresorbierbares, mikrochirurgisches Instrument zur nahtlosen Anastomose

Entwicklung eines neuartigen mikrochirurgischen Instruments mit bioresorbierbaren Materialien für einfachere, schnellere und sicherere nahtlose Anastomose

Projektleiter/in: Tobias Moser

Projektteam: Men Duri Coray, Vera Luginbühl

Projektbeginn: 01.01.2013

Every year, millions of anastomoses are carried out in the human body. Today, such surgeries usually connect two structures by sewing these together. This time-consuming process burdens medical doctors, patients & health care costs. The main goal of the proposed project is thus to develop a prototype of a novel microsurgical kit for easier, faster & more secure sutureless anastomosis. Meteso (patent holder) requires research expertise in materials, manufacturing & in-vivo/vitro assessments.
